I'm about to share it all. No website needed, no waiting 6 months for SEO results to kick in. Here's how to do good parasite SEO, to rank X posts, Instagram, etc. at #1 on commercial keywords on Google.

## Phase 1: Choosing your parasite platforms

Parasite SEO means using the authority of well-known sites to rank quickly on Google. But not all sites are equal.

Platforms that work in 2026:

- **X (Twitter)**: DR 97, indexed in 24-48h. Get X Premium ($5/month) for long posts
- **Instagram**: DR 95, excellent for visuals. 2200 characters max
- **Reddit**: DR 91, boosted by the Google deal. Create your own subreddit or use existing ones
- **LinkedIn**: DR 99, perfect for B2B. LinkedIn articles rank very well
- **GitHub**: DR 100, the most powerful but technical to set up
- **YouTube**: DR 100, videos often appear at position 0

My strategy: I always start with X and Instagram. Less competition than the big media outlets, and you can add direct links.

Watch out for declining platforms: LinkedIn Pulse gets indexed less and less, and Quora only works internationally.

Quick setup:

- Create accounts with your real identity (no fakes)
- Optimize bios with your main keywords
- Add backlinks to your profiles from your sites (authority boost)

On X (the most effective):

Winning format: Long post (not article, Google no longer indexes X articles correctly).

- First sentence = SEO Title: Google takes the first words as the title
- Bold structure: Replace H2s with bold text (Premium feature)
- Impactful content: 300-500 words, bullet points, no fluff
- Image + 2 hashtags max: Visual impact and theming
- Naturally integrated link: Not at the end of the post, in the middle

Sample template:

> 🔥 Wise promo code: -€20 on your international transfers **Why Wise crushes traditional banks:** → Real exchange rate with no markup → Transfers in 24h vs 3-5 days → Free multi-currency account **My takeaway after 2 years of use:** \[Concrete details\] 💰 Code: WISE20 → €20 free Link: \[short url\] [#Wise](https://x.com/search?q=%23Wise&src=hashtag_click) [#Banking](https://x.com/search?q=%23Banking&src=hashtag_click)

On Instagram:

- Nice photo/video as a hook
- Text of 2200 characters max
- Non-clickable but copyable link
- QR code in the image if possible

## Phase 4: Optimizing for fast indexing

Forcing indexing:

- Correct URL: Watch out for redirects, verify the URL responds with 200
- Indexing tool: Use a fast indexer (the one given by [@Pirote](https://x.com/@Pirote) on Telegram)
- Initial traffic: Send a few visitors in the first hours

Platform specifics:

- Facebook: Click on the post date → Private browsing → Retrieve the canonical URL with Link Redirect Trace (Chrome extension)
- YouTube: Use the share URL, verify it responds with 200
- Reddit: Direct, no URL problems

Boosting your posts:

- Backlinks to the post: Web profiles, ninja linking
- Initial engagement: A few likes/comments in the first hour
- Traffic: 50-100 visitors via Facebook ads or otherwise

Results: Indexing in 2-24h, visible ranking within 48-72h.

Scaling:

- 1 person: 5-10 posts/week, €500-1000/month
- Automated: 30-50 posts/week, €2000-3000/month
- With a VA: 100+ posts/week, €5000+/month

## Phase 5: Monetizing effectively

Monetization strategies:

1. Direct affiliation (the most profitable)
- SaaS: 20-50% commission
- Courses: 30-50% (often €100-500 per sale)
- Services: 10-30%
1. Referrals
- Banks: €50-150 per referral
- Neobanks: €30-80
- Crypto: €10-50
1. CPA (Cost Per Action)
- Sign-ups: €5-20
- Downloads: €1-5
- Free trials: €10-50

My current breakdown:

- 60% promo codes/affiliation
- 30% bank referrals
- 10% miscellaneous CPA

Tracking and optimization:

- UTM parameters on all links
- Facebook/Google conversion pixels
- Google Analytics 4 for tracking

Conversion optimization:

- A/B tests on hooks
- QR codes for mobile
- Short and memorable links
- Urgent call-to-action ("Limited offer")

⚠️ **Mistakes everyone makes**

- **Mass spam** → Google and the platforms detect it. Better to have 2-3 quality posts than 20 junk posts
- **Duplicate content** → Adapt content per platform. Same idea, different format, complete rewrite
- **Ignoring guidelines** → Each platform has its rules. Respecting them = longevity
- **No tracking** → Track positions, clicks, conversions. Without data, no optimization
- **Fake profiles** → Use your real identity. More credible and less risk of being banned
- **Focusing on volume** → Better to rank on 5 profitable queries than 50 useless ones
- **Neglecting the image** → Visuals boost engagement and CTR. ChatGPT/Midjourney to create them
- **Forgetting mobile** → 80% of traffic is mobile. Test the experience on smartphone

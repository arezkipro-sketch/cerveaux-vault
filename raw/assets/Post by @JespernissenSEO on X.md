ChatGPT is responsible for more than 78% of all AI referred traffic...

According to a study from SE Ranking, ChatGPT is the number one place to be, if you want a part of the AI traffic..

Perplexity is number two on the list with 9.33% and Gemini number three with 6.85%

Even though thats very interesting, the AI platforms only deliver 0.33% of the global web traffic.

Thats doubled in the last year, but its still very very low, when compared to traffic from traditional search engines.

So dont forget SEO yet...

I have heard anecdotal evidence from LLM optimization experts that traffic from the AI converts to a much higher degree, which is why you should combine your traditional SEO work with activities that target your visibility in the LLMs...

# Storytelling

**Definition:** Persuasion through narrative that places the *prospect* as the hero. Emotion + identification move people where rational argument doesn't. → [[academie-storytelling]]

## What we know
- **Why it works:** stories trigger dopamine/oxytocin → proximity (reader projects in), memorisation (mental images), engagement, faster action. → [[academie-storytelling]]
- **4 pillars:** identifiable hero (= the prospect) · central conflict/pain · narrative arc (situation → problem → quest → resolution) · convincing resolution where the offer becomes obvious. → [[academie-storytelling]]
- **Techniques:** engage the senses, before/after contrasts, dialogue, metaphors. → [[academie-storytelling]]
- **Mistakes:** too long, disconnected from the offer, useless detail, no emotional link. → [[academie-storytelling]]
- **× SEO:** raises read time, shares, conversion = positive ranking signals when paired with structured content. → [[academie-storytelling]]

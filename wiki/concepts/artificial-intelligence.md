# Artificial Intelligence (IA)

**Definition:** The capacity of a machine to imitate human intelligence — to learn, reason, solve problems, and make autonomous decisions. AI is **not a single technology** but an umbrella covering [[machine-learning]], [[deep-learning]], [[natural-language-processing]], computer vision, and more → [[apercu-ia-guide]].

## What we know
- "AI is not magic — it's mathematics, data, and a lot of compute. But the result is almost magical." → [[apercu-ia-guide]]
- Output quality is bounded by **data quality**: biased data → biased AI → [[apercu-ia-guide]].
- High deployment cost (data, infra, talent, time); recommended path for SMEs is to **start small with existing tools** before building custom solutions → [[apercu-ia-guide]].
- Regulation lags the technology; EU **AI Act** is in progress but ethics/privacy/liability remain a grey zone → [[apercu-ia-guide]].

## Relations
- Parent of [[machine-learning]], [[deep-learning]], [[natural-language-processing]].
- Drives [[ai-marketing]], [[ai-overviews]], [[ai-search-engines]], [[ai-seo]], [[generative-engine-optimization]].

## Tensions / open questions
- Liability when an AI makes a bad decision is unresolved.
- Marketing-flavored sources tend to overstate ROI (e.g. "+20% conversion") without citable evidence.

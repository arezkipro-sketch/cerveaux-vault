# Local SEO

**Definition:** The practice of optimizing a business's online presence to rank in location-specific searches — primarily Google Maps (map pack) and local organic results. Core levers: [[google-business-profile]], NAP consistency, local backlinks, location pages, reviews, and local content.

## What we know

### Core visibility mechanism
- **Map pack (3-pack)**: the 3 Google Business Profile listings shown above organic results for local queries. Position 1 = ~36 % clicks; position 2 = ~24 % → [[onelegchris-multiple-gbp]].
- **Two-channel visibility**: local SEO requires both map pack presence (GBP-driven) AND local organic rankings (traditional SEO-driven).
- Not the same as national/informational SEO — local intent queries differ in format, conversion, and ranking signals.

### Highest-value page type: high-intent emergency/service pages
- Pages targeting "[service] [city] same-day / emergency / near me" = customers in active distress = highest commercial conversion intent → [[bloggersarvesh-hvac-case-study]].
- These pages + GBP = complete local SEO coverage for a service area.
- Example: HVAC contractor → "AC repair [city] same-day" → 3.2 M$ outcome → [[bloggersarvesh-hvac-case-study]].

### Geo-expansion: multiple GBP profiles
- Single GBP = ~4-5 mile radius coverage → [[onelegchris-multiple-gbp]].
- Second GBP with overlapping radius = dual map pack presence = 60 %+ click share before competitors → [[onelegchris-multiple-gbp]].
- Applies only to genuinely distinct service-area locations.

### GBP as primary local signal
- Full optimization covers: categories, attributes, services section, description, reviews, posts, photos → [[google-business-profile]].
- Review velocity (reviews/month) matters more than total count → [[bloggersarvesh-claude-local-seo]].
- Neighborhood-specific content in posts and reviews = geographic relevance signals.

### Local backlinks
- Real local backlinks (local news sites, directories, community sponsors) = durable domain authority → [[bloggersarvesh-hvac-case-study]].
- Contrast: PBN links (black-hat) = risk without locality signal → [[jotaro-seo-pbn]].

### Internal linking for local sites
- Service × location pages interlinked → distributes PageRank, improves crawl → [[maillage-interne]].
- Each service-area page targets one [service] + [city] combination (1 keyword = 1 page rule) → [[keyword-cannibalization]].

### Local SEO × AI Overviews
- GBP products and business info can appear directly in Google AI Overviews for local queries → [[irentdumpsters-google-ai-guide]].
- AI optimization IS SEO (same Google systems) → no separate local AI playbook; standard local signals apply.
- GBP media (photos, videos) mentioned as AI visibility factor → [[irentdumpsters-google-ai-guide]].

### Claude-powered local SEO workflow
- 8-prompt Claude Code system for GBP audit: categories → attributes → reviews → posts → services → description → photos (4-week order) → [[bloggersarvesh-claude-local-seo]].

## Case study: HVAC
- Investment: 27 k$ / 12 months. Revenue: 3.2 M$.
- Strategy: emergency service pages + internal linking + GBP + local content + real backlinks → [[bloggersarvesh-hvac-case-study]].

## Relations
- Depends on [[google-business-profile]] as primary signal layer.
- Overlaps [[ai-seo]] via GBP in AI Overview responses.
- Content strategy follows [[search-intent]] (transactional/emergency intent first).
- Internal architecture follows [[maillage-interne]].
- Keyword strategy follows [[keyword-cannibalization]] (1 city = 1 page per service).

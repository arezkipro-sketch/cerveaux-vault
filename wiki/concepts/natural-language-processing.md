# Natural Language Processing (NLP)

**Definition:** The branch of [[artificial-intelligence]] dealing with machines understanding and producing human language. Listed as one of AI's core component technologies alongside [[machine-learning]], [[deep-learning]], and computer vision → [[apercu-ia-guide]].

## What we know
- Enables voice assistants and chatbots that handle thousands of conversations simultaneously, answer common questions, and qualify leads → [[apercu-ia-guide]].
- Foundation for LLM-driven tools (ChatGPT) and for [[ai-search-engines]] / [[generative-engine-optimization]].

## Relations
- Subset of [[artificial-intelligence]]; closely tied to [[deep-learning]] in modern systems.
- Powers chatbots in [[ai-marketing]].

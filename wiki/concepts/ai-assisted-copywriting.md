# AI-Assisted Copywriting

**Definition:** Using LLMs (e.g. ChatGPT) as a copywriting *assistant* — to generate ideas, variants and structure faster — while strategy, originality and human review stay with the writer. → [[academie-chatgpt-textes-vente]]

## What we know
- **Best uses:** title/hook ideas, benefit bullets, restructure with AIDA/PAS/FAB, **simulate the client's voice** (an AI shortcut for [[mirror-effect]]), generate A/B variants. → [[academie-chatgpt-textes-vente]]
- **7-prompt sales-page workflow:** audience pains → benefits → 5 hooks → page structure → social-proof angles → 5 CTAs → 10 objections + rebuttals. → [[academie-prompts-chatgpt-page-vente]]
- **Limits:** AI doesn't replace strategy (needs precise prompts), originality stays key (support not substitute), human verification mandatory for coherence/authenticity. → [[academie-chatgpt-textes-vente]]

## Relations
- Output quality depends on [[prompt-engineering]] (clarity/specificity/context/role) → [[prompt-chatgpt]].
- Speeds up [[sales-page-structure]] (the 7 prompts map to its sections); "simulate client voice" = [[mirror-effect]].
- Ethics caveat: AI-suggested testimonials must be made real → [[ethical-copywriting]]; human-review stance mirrors [[ai-seo]] / [[semrush-ai-seo-beginner-guide]].

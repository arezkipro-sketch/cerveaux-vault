# Query Fan-Out

**Definition:** How AI search (Google AI Mode) answers — it decomposes one query into multiple **sub-queries**, retrieves results per sub-query, then synthesises an answer. Covering those sub-questions improves the odds of being cited. → [[semrush-query-fan-out]]

## What we know
- AI Mode "fans out" a query → many sub-queries → per-sub-query retrieval → synthesis. → [[semrush-query-fan-out]]
- Implication: structure content to answer the likely sub-questions, not just the head query. → [[semrush-query-fan-out]]

## Relations
- The retrieval mechanism behind [[google-ai-mode]] / [[ai-overviews]] citation; act on it via [[question-keywords]], [[people-also-ask]], [[topic-clusters]] (cover sub-topics); corroborated by Gemini sub-query decomposition in [[alexgroberman-62pct-beyond-top10]].
- A GEO tactic within [[ai-seo]] / [[generative-engine-optimization]].

## Tensions / open questions
- Single experiment; directional. AI retrieval internals remain partly opaque.

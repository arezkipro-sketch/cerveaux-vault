# Email Copywriting

**Definition:** Writing emails that sell — a direct channel into the prospect's inbox with no algorithmic filter. A 4-step method produces a selling email in under 30 minutes. → [[academie-emails-qui-vendent]]

## What we know
- **Subject line** decides the open (benefit/curiosity/urgency): "Comment doubler vos rendez-vous en 30 jours" beats "Newsletter de septembre". → [[academie-emails-qui-vendent]]
- **Opener** = a handshake: question, striking stat, or short story the reader recognises. → [[academie-emails-qui-vendent]]
- **Body = benefits not features**, concise and rhythmic, one idea per paragraph; interest → desire. → [[academie-emails-qui-vendent]]
- **Proof** placed strategically, not overloaded; **CTA** clear, benefit-worded, repeated (esp. at end). → [[academie-emails-qui-vendent]]
- **4-step method:** pain/desire → solution → concrete proof → strong CTA. → [[academie-emails-qui-vendent]]

## Relations
- A compressed [[sales-page-structure]]; subject = [[headline]], opener = [[hook]], uses [[social-proof]] + [[call-to-action]].
- The MOFU/BOFU nurture engine of [[sales-funnel]] (sequence design = 5-7 emails / 10-14 days lives there).
- The writing craft inside the [[email-marketing]] channel; powers the [[newsletter]] editorial product.

## Tensions / open questions
- Sequence strategy detailed on [[sales-funnel]]. Subject-line emphasis: ~30% of emails read <2s, ~9s avg (Litmus); email ROI ~$36/$1.

## As a career/role
- **Email copywriter** is a specialised role: FR freelance ~€30-70k/yr (€50-1000+/email by level), some billing on results. A service [[copywriting-niches|niche]] of direct-response [[copywriting-types]]. → [[academie-email-copywriter]]

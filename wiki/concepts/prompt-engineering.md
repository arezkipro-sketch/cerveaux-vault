# Prompt Engineering

**Definition:** The skill of formulating instructions for an AI (e.g. [[chatgpt]]) so the output is precise, relevant, and actionable. Output quality is bounded by prompt quality — a strategic, not merely technical, competence → [[prompt-chatgpt]].

## What we know
- **Three levers** → [[prompt-chatgpt]]: **clarity**, **specificity**, **context** (audience, objective, tone, scenario, format, length).
- **General → detailed:** adding audience + goal + timeframe + format converts broad answers into actionable ones → [[prompt-chatgpt]].
- **Role prompting:** "Imagine you are a [role]…" produces sector-adapted, more relevant answers → [[prompt-chatgpt]].
- **Productivity:** good prompts cut iterations, save time, surface deeper insight; prompt quality ≈ the **ROI of AI adoption** → [[prompt-chatgpt]].
- Evidence: accuracy improves with specific/structured prompts (ResearchGate); 70% of ChatGPT use is personal (ITPro/OpenAI) → [[prompt-chatgpt]].

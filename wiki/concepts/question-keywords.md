# Question Keywords

**Definition:** Search queries phrased as questions (who/what/where/when/why/how, etc.). Rising with voice search; targeting them wins visibility, relevant traffic and niche authority. → [[semrush-question-keywords]]

## What we know
- **What:** queries with a question indicator; most-searched globally = "que regarder". → [[semrush-question-keywords]]
- **Why:** growing with voice search + Google's push for specific answers. → [[semrush-question-keywords]]
- **Find via:** Keyword Magic "Questions" filter, online communities, Google autocomplete, "People Also Ask". → [[semrush-question-keywords]]

## Relations
- A sub-type of [[keyword-research]]; feeds FAQ content + answer-style writing for [[aeo]] / [[ai-overviews]]; voice angle ties [[mobile-seo]].
- Answer-style targeting overlaps the FAQ rule in [[article-structure]].

# Pinterest Ads

**Definition:** [[pinterest]]'s ad platform — sponsored pins shown natively in feed/search/boards by **intention** (keywords, interests, behavior). Inspiration-based advertising that reaches users *early* in the buying journey, while they plan and compare → [[pinterest-ads]].

## What makes it different
- Funnel position: Google captures hot intent, Facebook interrupts, **Pinterest accompanies active reflection** → [[pinterest-ads]].
- **Long content lifespan** — a pin drives traffic for months (vs a Meta ad ~48h); it's a visual search engine → [[pinterest-ads]].
- Ads feel like useful suggestions, not interruptions → [[pinterest-ads]].

## Formats & targeting
- Formats: sponsored pins (image/video), carousel, Idea Pins, product collections, shopping/catalog ads.
- Targeting by **intention** > demographics: keywords, interests, behavior, lookalike, retargeting.
→ [[pinterest-ads]]

## Performance & fit
- Strong ROAS in decor, e-commerce, tourism, fashion, beauty, real estate, education; lower CPC than FB/IG; more qualified traffic; better recall → [[pinterest-ads]].
- **Fit:** considered/visual/emotional purchases. **Not** for impulse/urgency products → [[pinterest-ads]].
- Creative: vertical readable images, before/after, real situations, constant [[ab-testing]].

## Relations
- A paid-ads channel beside [[display-advertising]]; upper/mid-funnel anticipation in the [[sales-funnel]].
- Intention targeting echoes [[search-intent]]; "build preference before purchase" supports [[brand-strategy]] / [[brand-authority]].
- Organic counterpart: [[pinterest]] in [[search-everywhere-optimization]].

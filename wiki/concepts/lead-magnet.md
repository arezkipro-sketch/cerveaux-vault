# Lead Magnet

**Definition:** A free resource (guide, checklist, mini-course, template) traded for an email address. Irresistible = solves one urgent problem, proves expertise, and leads naturally to the next funnel step. → [[academie-lead-magnet]]

## What we know
- **3 steps:** (1) identify the audience's urgent problem (blockers, repeated mistakes, the quick win wanted) (2) craft a clear desirable promise — catchy title + specific benefit + measurable result + simple method/delay (3) structure valuable content: brief credible intro → step/point body (AIDA/PAS/FAB + bullets) → resources/templates → logical CTA. → [[academie-lead-magnet]]
- **Promise example:** "Checklist: 10 étapes pour structurer votre première page de vente"; avoid vague "Guide du copywriting". → [[academie-lead-magnet]]
- **Promote:** embed in top blog posts (internal links), pop-up/banner, LinkedIn/newsletter, webinar bonus. → [[academie-lead-magnet]]

## Relations
- The TOFU email-capture asset of [[sales-funnel]]; delivered via a [[landing-page]] capture page.
- Promise built with [[headline]] + [[hook]]; content uses [[copywriting-frameworks]] + [[product-bullet-points]]; ends on [[call-to-action]].

## Tensions / open questions
- Best format per audience (checklist vs mini-course) not benchmarked.

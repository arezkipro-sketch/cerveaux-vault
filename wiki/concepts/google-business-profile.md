# Google Business Profile (GBP)

**Definition:** Google's free local business listing platform — controls how a business appears in Google Maps, the local map pack (3-pack), and increasingly in AI Overview responses for local queries. The primary signal layer for [[local-seo]].

## What we know

### Why GBP matters
- Map pack position 1 = ~36 % click share; position 2 = ~24 % → [[onelegchris-multiple-gbp]].
- GBP products and business info can surface directly inside Google AI Overview responses for local queries → [[irentdumpsters-google-ai-guide]].
- Businesses with GBP photos get **42 % more direction requests** and **35 % more click-throughs** (Google data) → [[bloggersarvesh-claude-local-seo]].

### Key GBP optimization levers (from [[bloggersarvesh-claude-local-seo]])

**Categories**
- Primary + secondary categories directly control which map pack searches trigger your listing.
- Wrong/missing secondary category = invisible for entire search segments.
- Adding one secondary category can trigger new searches within days.
- Audit: map competitor categories → ranking positions → identify gaps.

**Attributes**
- Tags: "veteran-owned", "free estimates", "24/7 availability", "wheelchair accessible", etc.
- Google matches attribute-specific searches (e.g. "24 hour plumber near me") against GBP attributes.
- All top competitors sharing an attribute = baseline market requirement, not differentiator.
- Two-for-one: ranking signal + CTR trust signal.

**Services section**
- Only GBP element where owner controls the full text.
- Each service should have 2-3 sentence description with keywords + area + benefit.
- Services on website not listed in GBP = invisible in map pack for those searches.

**Description**
- Max 750 characters. Testable asset — 3 versions recommended (keyword-focused, conversion-focused, balanced).
- Must include primary keywords + service areas + USP.

**Reviews**
- Review velocity (reviews/month) matters more than total count or star rating.
- Responding to reviews improves local ranking (Google confirmed).
- Response templates: keyword-rich responses mentioning service + city = 120 pieces of keyword-dense GBP content/year at 10 reviews/month.
- Keywords and locations mentioned in reviews = organic ranking signal from customers.

**Posts**
- GBP posts expire after 7 days. Consistent posting = active listing signal → preferred placement.
- Most competitors don't post → low-competition differentiation opportunity.
- Neighborhood-specific posts build location relevance: "just completed [service] in [neighborhood]".
- Recommended cadence: 2-3 posts/week.

**Photos**
- Consistency over volume: 3-5 quality photos/week > 50 photos dumped then nothing.
- Most valuable types: before-after, team on job sites, trucks in specific neighborhoods, completed installs.
- Regular uploads signal active business to Google.

### Multi-profile strategy
- Single GBP covers ~4-5 mile radius → [[onelegchris-multiple-gbp]].
- Two listings with slightly overlapping radius = both appear in map pack = 60 %+ click capture.
- **Risk**: permitted only for genuinely distinct locations or service-area businesses. Same-address duplication risks suspension.

### GBP in AI context
- Google's own guidance confirms GBP content can appear in AI Overview responses for local queries → [[irentdumpsters-google-ai-guide]].
- Extends [[ai-seo]] to local businesses: GBP = structured data layer that AI can read.

### 4-week optimization order (from [[bloggersarvesh-claude-local-seo]])
1. Categories + attributes (fastest ranking impact).
2. Services section + description (keyword-rich indexable text).
3. Review velocity target + response templates.
4. Posts calendar + photo plan.

## Relations
- Foundation of [[local-seo]].
- GBP content surfaces in [[ai-overviews]] for local queries → [[ai-seo]] intersection.
- Connects to [[e-e-a-t]]: review volume + responses + posting activity = trust signals.

## Tensions / open questions
- Multi-profile ToS ambiguity: Google permits distinct locations but boundary cases unclear.
- GBP attribute impact on ranking is documented qualitatively; no public quantitative study of attribute-specific ranking lift *(unsourced)*.
- How far does Claude Code/AI automation of GBP audit extend? Dependent on browser-enabled AI access.

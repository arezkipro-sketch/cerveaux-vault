# Content Calendar

**Definition:** A document that plans and visualises content publishing — what/when/where/who — across blog, social and email over weeks or months. → [[semrush-content-calendar-examples]]

## What we know
- **Tracks:** what content, when + where it publishes, who's responsible, links to assets. → [[semrush-content-calendar-examples]]
- **Built in:** spreadsheets, calendar apps, or tools (CoSchedule). → [[semrush-content-calendar-examples]]

## Relations
- The publishing-cadence layer for [[marketing-tactics]] and content channels (blog, [[instagram-copywriting]], [[email-copywriting]]); a sales-oriented editorial calendar variant exists (TODO reinforce).

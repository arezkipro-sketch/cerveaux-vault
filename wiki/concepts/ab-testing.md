# A/B Testing

**Definition:** Comparing two (or more) variants of a page/element against real users to see which performs better — the empirical backstop behind "always test" advice across copywriting and SEO → [[website-redesign]].

## What we know
- In a redesign, A/B tests give insight on layouts, **CTAs**, and even **titles/headlines** before/at launch, avoiding bad surprises → [[website-redesign]].

## Relations
- The verification step for [[headline]] (test 3 titles), [[call-to-action]] ("always test"), [[landing-page]], [[sales-page-structure]], and [[ai-assisted-copywriting]] variants.
- Part of [[website-redesign]] step 7 and continuous [[seo-audit]]/CRO optimization; AI may auto-generate design variants for testing (per [[core-web-vitals]] future trends).

## Tensions / open questions
- Needs sufficient traffic/time for significance — not addressed in the source.

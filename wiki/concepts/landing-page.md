# Landing Page

**Definition:** A page built for ONE goal — conversion (signup, booking, quote) — unlike a normal multi-purpose web page. Built on a fixed structure and stripped of distraction. → [[academie-landing-page]]

## What we know
- **Essential elements:** catchy title (the promise) · clear subtitle (offer + value) · impactful visual · social proof · visible CTA repeated at strategic spots. → [[academie-landing-page]]
- **Title** speaks to the reader with a concrete benefit; **CTA** visible/contrasted/benefit-worded ("Réservez votre appel gratuit"). → [[academie-landing-page]]
- **Mistakes:** too much info, weak CTA, no proof, non-mobile design. → [[academie-landing-page]]
- **Advanced levers:** scarcity, authority, personalisation by traffic source, storytelling. → [[academie-landing-page]]

# App Store Optimization (ASO)

**Definition:** Optimizing an app's listing in the App Store / Google Play so users discover and install it — "as important as SEO" because users go directly to app stores to install → [[search-everywhere-optimization]].

## What we know
- Tactics → [[search-everywhere-optimization]]: optimized titles + descriptions, regular listing updates, clear attractive visuals, highlight new features, engage users, monitor reviews + overall rating.
- "Your app listing is your sales page."

## Relations
- A platform of [[search-everywhere-optimization]] (e-commerce/discovery category); the marketplace analogue of [[landing-page]] + [[seo-copywriting]].

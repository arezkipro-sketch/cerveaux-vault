# Email Marketing

**Definition:** The email channel as a marketing system — owned audience, direct relationship, free of platform algorithms. Spans both **promotional email** (punctual, conversion-focused) and the recurring **[[newsletter]]** (editorial, relationship-focused) → [[newsletter-guide]].

## What we know
- **Promotional email vs newsletter** → [[newsletter-guide]]: promo = direct sales, short-term ("salesman knocking"); newsletter = long-term nurture ("weekly consultant"); top firms run both complementarily.
- **List building:** quality > quantity; give a real subscribe reason; tactics = forms/landing pages/popups, [[lead-magnet]]s, webinars/events, social integration → [[newsletter-guide]].
- **GDPR consent (mandatory):** clear opt-in, **double opt-in** (confirmation email), easy unsubscribe — also a trust lever; never buy lists → [[newsletter-guide]].
- **Automation + segmentation = the pillars:** welcome sequences, behavior-triggered flows, 60-day-inactive re-engagement; segment by profile / behavior / funnel stage → [[newsletter-guide]].
- Benchmarks cited: ~42.35% avg open rate; 69% of consumers prefer email for brand contact → [[newsletter-guide]] *(vendor data)*.

## Relations
- Parent channel of [[newsletter]]; written with [[email-copywriting]]; feeds [[lead-generation]] and [[sales-funnel]].
- "Owned audience" logic contrasts with algorithm-dependent [[social-media-strategy]].

## Tensions / open questions
- GDPR specifics are EU-centric; other regions differ (CAN-SPAM, etc.) — not covered.

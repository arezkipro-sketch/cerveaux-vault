# AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)

**Definition:** Optimizing content to appear in or be cited by AI-generated responses from answer engines (ChatGPT, Google AI Mode, AI Overviews, Perplexity, voice search). AEO extends classical SEO — same foundation, different success metrics and discovery surface.

## What we know

### AEO vs SEO
| Dimension | SEO | AEO |
|---|---|---|
| Goal | Rank in SERP links | Be cited/mentioned IN AI-generated answers |
| Discovery surface | SERP links, snippets, PAA, featured boxes | AI responses, citations, brand mentions in AI |
| User behavior | Browse results, click to visit | Read AI answer; may or may not click through |
| Success metric | Ranking position, CTR, organic traffic | Mention frequency, citation share, brand SoV in AI |

→ [[semrush-aeo-vs-seo]]

### Scale of "answer engines"
- **77 %** of ChatGPT US users treat it as a search engine → [[semrush-aeo-vs-seo]].
- **~60 %** of consumers use AI tools for shopping research → [[semrush-aeo-vs-seo]].
- ChatGPT: 700 M weekly active users (as of 2025) → [[semrush-ai-stats-2026]].

### How AEO and SEO work together
- Strong SEO builds domain authority → feeds AEO (AI trusts authoritative domains).
- Authoritative domain + extractable specific content = optimal for both.
- AEO doesn't replace SEO; it extends it — Google still handles 5T searches/year.

### What drives AEO specifically (beyond classic SEO)
- **Original data and first-hand experience**: AI cites unique content over generic → [[citation-absorption]].
- **Named authors with credentials**: credibility signal for AI systems.
- **Community validation** (Reddit, Wikipedia, forums): AI trust signal → [[semrush-ai-visibility-study-findings]].
- **Brand cooccurrence**: brand appearing near target keywords across multiple sources → AI associates them → [[semrush-optimize-ai-2026]].
- **Content freshness**: 90 % of AI-cited content updated in last 3 years → [[semrush-rank-in-ai-search]].

### Platform differentiation (critical for AEO targeting)
- Perplexity: highest overlap with traditional Google top-10 → easiest to target with classic SEO.
- ChatGPT: lowest overlap with traditional Google → most independent signal system.
- Google AI Mode: 80 % weight in Semrush's weighted SoV metric → primary target → [[google-ai-mode]].

## AEO ≈ GEO ≈ AI SEO — terminology note
- **AEO** = Semrush's preferred term.
- **GEO ([[generative-engine-optimization]])** = academic + Semrush alternative term for same concept → [[semrush-geo-definition]].
- **AI SEO** = broader term encompassing both (1) optimizing for AI citation and (2) using AI tools to do SEO → [[ai-seo]].
- All three point to the same practice; use interchangeably.

# Idea Validation

**Definition:** Deciding which business idea to pursue — eliminate false good ideas, validate real market demand, and pick a profitable one *before building a product*. The blocker is indecision, not lack of ideas. → [[jeremy-allard-bonne-idee]]

## What we know
- Endless idea-switching = being your own brake; an imperfect decision beats perfect deliberation. → [[jeremy-allard-bonne-idee]]
- Validate demand in the real market (talk to prospects) rather than building first. → [[jeremy-allard-bonne-idee]]

## Relations
- Validate via [[lead-generation]] + [[usp]] (talk to real prospects); decision paralysis links [[cognitive-biases]]; precedes [[ebook-selling]] / product creation.

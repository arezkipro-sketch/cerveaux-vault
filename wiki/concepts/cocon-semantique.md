# Cocon Sémantique (Semantic Web Cocoon)

**Definition:** A French SEO content architecture method — structuring a site's pages so that thematically related articles (pages filles / daughter pages) interlink and flow link equity to a central money page (page mère / mother page), building topical authority and improving SERP visibility for the whole cluster.

## What we know
- **Structure**: a page mère (product/category page for the primary keyword) surrounded by pages filles (supporting articles targeting long-tail variations) — all within the same thematic → [[jotaro-seo-long-tail-strategy]].
- **Mechanics**: pages filles rank on long-tail keywords, capture qualified traffic, and each links back to the page mère — concentrating [[link-juice]] and authority on the commercial page → [[jotaro-seo-long-tail-strategy]].
- **Rule**: page mère and pages filles **must share the same thematic** — cross-topic linking dilutes the cocoon effect → [[jotaro-seo-long-tail-strategy]].
- It is the structured output of a [[long-tail-keywords]] strategy: many niche articles all pointing inward → [[jotaro-seo-long-tail-strategy]].

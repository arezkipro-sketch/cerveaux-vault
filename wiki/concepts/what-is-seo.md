# What is SEO (fundamentals)

**Definition:** Search Engine Optimization — making a site more visible in search engines *without paying for ads*, to earn organic rankings, brand awareness and traffic. → [[semrush-what-is-seo]]

## What we know
- **4 pillars of a full SEO strategy:** → [[semrush-what-is-seo]]
  - **Keyword research** — what the audience searches → [[keyword-research]]
  - **On-page SEO** — pages + content → [[article-structure]], [[topic-clusters]]
  - **Off-page SEO** — links/reputation → [[off-page-seo]], [[backlinks]]
  - **Technical SEO** — performance + accessibility → [[seo-audit]], [[mobile-seo]], [[site-health]]
- **vs SEM:** SEM = paid + organic; SEO = the organic half. → [[sem]]

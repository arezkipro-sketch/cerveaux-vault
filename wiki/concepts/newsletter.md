# Newsletter

**Definition:** A recurring, *owned* editorial email channel — a "product" with a format, editorial line, and objective — that nurtures a relationship over the long term and is not subject to social-media algorithms → [[newsletter-guide]].

## What we know
- Edge over social: **control, predictability, preference** — you speak directly to people who opted in → [[newsletter-guide]].
- Function: educate the audience, sell non-intrusively (trust first), and build brand authority → [[newsletter-guide]].
- Distinct from [[email-marketing]]: the newsletter nurtures long-term ("weekly consultant"); promotional email converts short-term ("salesman at the door"). Best practice uses both → [[newsletter-guide]].
- Built on a quality opt-in list, GDPR consent, automation, and segmentation (see [[email-marketing]]) → [[newsletter-guide]].

## Relations
- A format within [[email-marketing]]; written with [[email-copywriting]] craft.
- Feeds [[lead-generation]] and the [[sales-funnel]]; grown via [[lead-magnet]]s and [[landing-page]] forms.

## Tensions / open questions
- Source pitches an agency build — DIY vs agency trade-off is presented one-sidedly.

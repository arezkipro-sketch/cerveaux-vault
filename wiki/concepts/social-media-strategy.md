# Social Media Strategy (what/when to post)

**Definition:** What and when to post for engagement. Evidence (1-yr study of top tech brands): **content type matters more than day/time**. → [[semrush-social-posting-study]]

## What we know
- Content types with most engagement ≠ those posted most often. → [[semrush-social-posting-study]]
- **Content type > timing**; combined, day/time has no significant engagement impact. Popular window (Tue-Thu, 9am-3pm) reflects volume, not engagement. → [[semrush-social-posting-study]]
- Caveat: tech brands, 2022 data — may not generalise.

## Social as search
- Social platforms are now **search engines** ([[instagram]], [[tiktok]], [[pinterest]]); ~40% of young users prefer IG/TikTok over Google. Social-search fundamentals: consistent profile names, complete profiles, keyword bios/posts, quality visuals, trend content → [[search-everywhere-optimization]].

## Relations
- Reprioritises [[content-calendar]] (what > when); engagement is the aim of [[instagram-copywriting]] + social [[copywriting-types]]; competitor benchmarking via [[competitor-analysis]].
- A category of [[search-everywhere-optimization]].

## Tensions / open questions
- Sector/recency limits; "consistency" advice elsewhere vs "type-over-timing" here — both can hold.

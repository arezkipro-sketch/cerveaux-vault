# Search Everywhere Optimization

**Definition:** The "new SEO": optimizing a brand's **organic presence across every platform its audience searches** — not just Google, but social, AI assistants, marketplaces, and emerging channels. A response to fragmented search → [[search-everywhere-optimization]].

## 5 platform categories
1. **Traditional search** — Google (90%+, ~⅔ web traffic), Bing (B2B): backlinks, technical, deep content; AI Overviews via E-E-A-T + expert citations.
2. **Social search** — [[instagram]]/Facebook, [[tiktok]] (commercial discovery; influences Google), [[pinterest]] (visual search). Fundamentals: consistent profiles, keyword bios, quality visuals, trends.
3. **AI search** — [[chatgpt]] et al; builds notoriety even without clicks → [[generative-engine-optimization]] (expert structured content, FAQ, schema, indexation).
4. **E-commerce** — Google Shopping + product schema; Amazon ([[amazon-funnel]]); App Stores ([[app-store-optimization]]).
5. **Underdog** — brand podcasts, voice search (write as people speak → [[question-keywords]]), forums ([[reddit]]/Quora) → [[reddit-seo]].
→ all from [[search-everywhere-optimization]]

## Why it matters
- Reach/notoriety, engagement, conversions, **algorithm-change resilience** (less single-platform dependence), larger SEO surface (Google indexes video/podcasts/social) → [[search-everywhere-optimization]].

## Cross-platform constant
- Strengthen **[[e-e-a-t]]** everywhere + **consistent brand identity** (same name/visuals/tone) → [[search-everywhere-optimization]].

## Implementation (5 steps)
- Identify audience search habits → adapt content per platform (same message, different formats) → data-driven (native analytics + GSC) → optimize local + voice → consistent brand identity → [[search-everywhere-optimization]].

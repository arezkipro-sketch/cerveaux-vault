# GEO Tools (AI-search visibility)

**Definition:** Tools that reveal and track a brand's visibility in AI search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Google AI Overviews, Bing AI) — AI citations, mentions, share-of-voice, sentiment, competitor comparison, and strategic recommendations. The "AI-era" analogue of classic SEO rank trackers → [[ai-visibility-tools]].

## Why they exist
- Traditional analytics can't measure **zero-click** AI exposure; **Gartner predicts traditional search volume −25% by 2026** → tracking AI presence becomes essential → [[ai-visibility-tools]].
- Best practice: **combine** AI-visibility data with classic metrics (views/positions/backlinks) for a full picture → [[ai-visibility-tools]].

## Common metrics
- Brand Visibility (mention count), Industry Rank, Top Prompts, Competitor Visibility, **Sentiment**, **Authority** by topic cluster, a single visibility/SoV score → [[ai-visibility-tools]].

## Tool landscape (2026)
- **[[ubersuggest]]** — in-suite, flat price + unlimited projects (SMB/agency). *(NP Digital's own.)*
- **[[profound]]** — enterprise; sentiment + competitive + authority; custom dashboards/alerts; pricey.
- **[[semrush]]** — in-suite; visibility score; ties AI citations to backlinks/rankings.
- **[[ahrefs]]** — backlink leader; **links↔AI-citation correlation** (what makes content citable).
- **[[scrunchai]]** — AI-visibility-only; real-time alerts; quality+frequency; **query simulation**.
→ all from [[ai-visibility-tools]]

## Choosing criteria
- Budget · team technical level · dedicated vs integrated · scalability (per-project limits vs unlimited) · data reliability (mature vs new) · update cadence → [[ai-visibility-tools]].

## Relations
- Measurement/tooling layer for [[ai-seo]], [[aeo]], [[generative-engine-optimization]], [[ai-overviews]], [[google-ai-mode]]; tracks the dynamics in [[citation-absorption]].
- AI-search analogue of [[copywriting-tools]] / classic SEO tools.

## Outils d'optimisation sémantique (contenu GEO)

Outils pour créer le contenu optimisé GEO (sémantique, structuration, lacunes) — distincts des trackers de visibilité :

| Outil | Fonction | Intérêt GEO |
|---|---|---|
| Surfer SEO | Optimisation sémantique on-page | Analyse champ lexical + recommandations |
| Frase | Recherche + rédaction assistée IA | Briefs + optimisation questions utilisateurs |
| MarketMuse | Analyse lacunes de contenu | Sujets manquants pour couvrir un thème |
| YourTextGuru | Optimisation sémantique francophone | Score d'optimisation + suggestions de termes |
| Semrush | Suite SEO complète | Suivi visibilité IA, audit technique |
| MarketBrew | Validation Schema.org | Implémentation structured data |

→ from [[natural-net-geo-guide-2026]]

## Tensions / open questions
- Vendor listicle bias (Semrush ranks its own #1; Neil Patel ranks Ubersuggest #1); the market is young and shifting.
- No cross-tool standard for "visibility score" yet.
- Distinction à maintenir : **trackers de mentions LLM** (Profound, Peec AI, ScrunchAI) vs **outils d'optimisation sémantique** (Surfer, Frase, MarketMuse).

# Brand Identity

**Definition:** What makes a company immediately recognizable — its **visual** elements (name, logo, colors, typography) and **verbal** elements (tone, communication style). A strategic tool, not just aesthetics → [[strategie-de-marque]].

## What we know
- Step 3 of building a [[brand-strategy]]: every element must reflect the intended personality and create a **coherent experience at each touchpoint** (site, social, campaigns) → [[strategie-de-marque]].
- Well-executed identity drives memorability + differentiation in saturated markets → [[strategie-de-marque]].

## Relations
- A component of [[brand-strategy]]; the consistent identity that [[search-everywhere-optimization]] and [[omnichannel]] require across channels.
- Visual/verbal craft overlaps [[color-psychology]], [[visual-copywriting]], and brand voice.

# SEM (Search Engine Marketing)

**Definition:** Boosting a site's search visibility through *both* paid ads (PPC, shown as "sponsored") and unpaid organic results (earned via SEO). Both are keyword- and intent-driven. → [[semrush-sem]]

## What we know
- **SEM = paid + organic.** Paid search = sponsored PPC results; SEO = unlabeled organic results. Using both maximises visibility, traffic, leads, revenue. → [[semrush-sem]]
- **Keyword + intent driven:** right keywords in the right places; success depends on matching **search intent**. → [[semrush-sem]]

## Relations
- The umbrella over SEO ([[off-page-seo]] + on-page) and paid search; intent-matching = [[search-intent]].
- Paid side connects to the ads world (negative keywords, bidding); organic side = the rest of this vault's SEO cluster.

## Tensions / open questions
- PPC mechanics (bidding, Quality Score) not detailed here.

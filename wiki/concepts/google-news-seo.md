# Google News SEO (Google Actualités)

**Definition:** A distinct Google SERP channel (Google Actualités) that surfaces recent, credible news articles based on freshness, relevance, and source credibility — accessible to any publisher who meets technical requirements and applies to be included.

## What we know
- **Algorithm criteria**: freshness (recency), relevance (query match), credibility (source trust signals) → [[jotaro-seo-google-news]].
- **SEO benefits**: qualified traffic (users actively seeking fresh info), authority credibility signal, competitive SERP advantage on trending topics → [[jotaro-seo-google-news]].
- **Classic SEO impact**: Google News presence → increased traffic → quality signals → improved SERP ranking → [[jotaro-seo-google-news]].

### Technical requirements
1. **Search Console registration**: submit Google News inclusion request, optimize site metadata → [[jotaro-seo-google-news]].
2. **Unique URL per article**: no duplicate content / no URL recycling → [[jotaro-seo-google-news]].
3. **Google News sitemap**: special XML sitemap with `<news:publication>`, `<news:publication_date>`, `<news:title>`, `<news:keywords>`, `<news:genres>` fields. Update continuously; **remove articles from sitemap 30 days after indexation** → [[jotaro-seo-google-news]].

## Relations
- Content-side: [[topical-authority]] + regular publishing (see [[jotaro-seo-content-strategy]]) = natural fit for Google News inclusion.
- Technical-side: unique URLs per article = no-duplicate rule aligns with [[canonical-tag]] best practices.
- Freshness emphasis contrasts with evergreen [[cocon-semantique]] strategy — Google News favors timely topical content.

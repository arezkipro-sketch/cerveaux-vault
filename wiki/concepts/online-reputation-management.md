# Online Reputation Management (ORM)

**Definition:** Monitoring and improving a brand's reputation across review sites, social and search — one negative review/post can damage a brand, so ORM centralises tracking + response. → [[semrush-orm-tools]]

## What we know
- ORM tools centralise monitoring + response across the many review platforms; goal = protect/improve brand reputation. → [[semrush-orm-tools]]

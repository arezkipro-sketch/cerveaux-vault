# People Also Ask (PAA)

**Definition:** A Google SERP feature showing questions related to the query, each answered from a specific page. Appearing there = extra visibility and a source of new question keywords. → [[semrush-people-also-ask]]

## What we know
- **What:** related questions near the SERP top; clicking reveals a short answer (paragraph/list/table/image/video) pulled from a page. → [[semrush-people-also-ask]]
- **Why:** dual visibility (classic + PAA); a keyword-idea mine. Find via SERP inspection, tools, autocomplete. → [[semrush-people-also-ask]]

## Relations
- The SERP-feature side of [[question-keywords]]; answer-style content for PAA feeds [[aeo]] / [[ai-overviews]] and the FAQ rule in [[article-structure]].

## Tensions / open questions
- Overlap with featured-snippet optimisation not fully separated.

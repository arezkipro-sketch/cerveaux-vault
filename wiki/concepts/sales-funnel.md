# Sales Funnel (Tunnel de vente)

**Definition:** The structured path that turns a stranger into a qualified prospect and then a client, across 3-5 stages. Unlike a brochure site (which informs), a funnel orients, reassures and pushes to action — addressing each visitor at their maturity stage. → [[academie-tunnel-de-vente]]

## What we know
- **3 stages (TOFU/MOFU/BOFU):** **TOFU** attract — SEO, social, ads, educational content + lead magnet (capture email). **MOFU** nurture — email sequences, case studies, testimonials, webinars (answer objections). **BOFU** convert — sales page, offer, guarantee, decisive CTA. → [[academie-tunnel-de-vente]]
- **6-step build:** define audience + problem → offer that solves it → capture page (one title/benefit/button) → email sequence (5-7 emails / 10-14 days) → sales page (AIDA/PAS) → optimise checkout. → [[academie-tunnel-de-vente]]
- **Stats:** single-CTA pages convert ~2.5× (HubSpot); ~20% of page text is read (Nielsen Norman); ~73% of leads not ready on first contact (MarketingSherpa). → [[academie-tunnel-de-vente]]
- **Mistakes:** too many steps, generic message, no follow-up, ignoring data. Iterate via A/B.

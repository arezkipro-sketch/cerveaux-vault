# Client Acquisition (copywriting business)

**Definition:** How a copywriter finds paying clients. Core principle: don't rely on one method (the "client-search trap") — try many tactics; volume of outreach → volume of clients; referrals compound over time. → [[frw-land-clients-35-tips]]

## What we know
- **Diversify tactics:** contact previous colleagues (fastest — they vouch for you), webinars, careful paid ads, branded social pages with valuable content, Quora/Medium value posts (no hard sell), networking groups, LinkedIn, portfolio newsletter + opt-in bonus, host online events, free 15-min evaluations. → [[frw-land-clients-35-tips]]
- **Free-consult rule:** give the *what* (spotted weak benefit/unclear CTA), never the *how* (the paid writing). → [[frw-land-clients-35-tips]]
- **Closing on discovery calls = energy, not portfolio/pricing:** shift from "pick-me" to confident leadership — lead the client, don't chase them. → [[copyposse-psychological-triggers]]

## Relations
- Applies the whole craft to the copywriter's *own* business: portfolio newsletter = [[lead-magnet]] + [[email-copywriting]]; value content builds [[brand-authority]] + [[social-proof]]; targeting uses [[copywriting-niches]] + [[usp]].
- The business-of-copywriting counterpart to the FRW career sources ([[nicki-krawczyk]]).

# Instagram Copywriting

**Definition:** Treating each Instagram caption as a short sales text — the photo stops the scroll, the words convert. Mobile-first, engagement-driven. → [[academie-instagram-captions]]

## What we know
- **Why unique:** short/dynamic (the lines before "voir plus" decide), emotion-seeking audience, algorithm rewards engagement (likes/comments/saves/shares). → [[academie-instagram-captions]]
- **Caption structure:** punchy hook → story/value → natural transition → clear CTA → 3-5 relevant hashtags. → [[academie-instagram-captions]]
- **7 tips:** clarity, storytelling, ask questions, emojis sparingly, line breaks/bullets, brand-consistent tone, test & analyse. → [[academie-instagram-captions]]
- **Mistakes:** just describing the photo, too many/generic hashtags, text walls, no CTA. → [[academie-instagram-captions]]

## Relations
- A short-form social application of [[hook]], [[storytelling]], [[call-to-action]], [[micro-copywriting]], framed by AIDA/PAS ([[copywriting-frameworks]]).
- Engagement-as-distribution parallels the SEO-signal angle in [[storytelling]].

## Tensions / open questions
- Hashtag strategy stated loosely; platform behaviour shifts over time.

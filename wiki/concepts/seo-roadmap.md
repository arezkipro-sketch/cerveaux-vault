# SEO Roadmap

**Definition:** A document formalising a step-by-step plan — tasks, timelines, owners — to improve a site's search performance toward business goals. → [[semrush-seo-roadmap]]

## What we know
- **What:** custom plan in a sheet/PM tool (e.g. 9-month). → [[semrush-seo-roadmap]]
- **Why:** breaks complex SEO into manageable tasks (keyword research → page optimisation vs vague "improve SEO"); prioritises high-impact work; aligns team + timelines. → [[semrush-seo-roadmap]]

## Relations
- Sequences the whole SEO cluster: [[keyword-research]] → on-page/[[topic-clusters]] → [[off-page-seo]]/[[backlinks]] → technical/[[seo-audit]]; measured via [[google-search-console]] + [[google-analytics-4]].
- The strategy/planning layer above the tactical SEO concepts.

## Tensions / open questions
- Horizon + sequencing are template-driven, not universal.

### Julien CTR — Taxonomie SEO en 15 catégories (2026-06-09)

Vision plus complète : Idée → Validation → Architecture → Mots Clés → Contenu → Technique → Indexation → Autorité → Signaux → Discover → GEO → Automatisation → Mesure → Optimisation → Croissance → Objectif (Machine SEO).

Apports clés vs Semrush :
- **Validation business** avant le SEO : coût du lead, valeur client, vitesse de monétisation
- **Google Discover** comme canal distinct
- **Automatisation SEO** (agents IA, audits massifs)
- **Revenu par page** comme métrique (pas seulement trafic)
- **Actifs qui composent** — vision long terme

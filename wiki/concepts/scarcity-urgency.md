# Scarcity & Urgency (FOMO levers)

**Definition:** The paired FOMO levers — **scarcity** (limited supply/access raises perceived value) and **urgency** (time pressure pushes action now). Powerful, but only ethical and effective when *real* and *justified*; fake versions erode trust. → [[academie-effet-rarete]], [[academie-urgence-sans-agressivite]]

## What we know
- **Scarcity, 3 forms:** quantity (limited seats/stock), time (clear deadline), access (VIP/private circle). → [[academie-effet-rarete]]
- **Urgency without aggression:** lead with value gained by acting now (bonus, early coaching), not loss; soft urgency via social proof ("2000+ already joined"); transparent justified deadlines; exclusive bonuses > threats. → [[academie-urgence-sans-agressivite]]
- **Ethics (hard rule):** justify the limit, positive framing, stay consistent — an "exceptional" offer that always returns is discredited. Tie scarcity/urgency to a clear buyer benefit. → both

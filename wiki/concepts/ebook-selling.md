# Ebook Selling

**Definition:** Turning a PDF into a product people buy, recommend and rebuy. Near-zero production cost, near-100% margin when sold direct — an asset that sells while you sleep. → [[academie-vendre-ebook]]

## What we know
- **Topic:** solve one precise, searched, payable problem; niche beats generalist. 3 filters: verifiable demand, demonstrable expertise, logical monetisation. → [[academie-vendre-ebook]]
- **Write:** structure as a kept promise (intro=problem → chapters=advances → conclusion=action plan); TOC, examples, visuals, pro layout; beta-read with 3-5 target readers. → [[academie-vendre-ebook]]
- **Price (FR):** €0-4.99 lead magnet · €5-19.99 practical guide · €20-97 program; price signals value; test then adjust. → [[academie-vendre-ebook]]
- **Platforms:** marketplaces (Amazon KDP 30-65% fee, visibility) vs direct (Gumroad/Podia/Systeme.io, keep 90-97%, owns client). Combine both. → [[academie-vendre-ebook]]
- **Sales page:** benefit title → problem hook → solution+results → social proof → repeated CTA → guarantee (F-scan, strongest top-left). → [[academie-vendre-ebook]]
- **Market:** FR e-book >€1.3B (SNE 2023), ~16M users by 2029 (Statista). → [[academie-vendre-ebook]]

# Machine Learning

**Definition:** A subset of [[artificial-intelligence]] that lets systems **learn from data without being explicitly programmed for each task**. Analogy: teaching a child to recognize dogs by showing many examples — the system generalizes, at massive scale → [[apercu-ia-guide]].

## What we know
- Identifies patterns/trends in data that humans would miss (e.g. marketing-campaign analysis, SEO keyword opportunities) → [[apercu-ia-guide]].
- Powers prediction tasks: demand forecasting, lead-conversion scoring, fraud detection → [[apercu-ia-guide]].

## Relations
- Subset of [[artificial-intelligence]]; [[deep-learning]] is a further subset.
- Underlies [[ai-marketing]] personalization and prediction.

## Tensions / open questions
- Inherits the data-bias problem of [[artificial-intelligence]].

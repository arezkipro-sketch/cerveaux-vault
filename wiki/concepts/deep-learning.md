# Deep Learning

**Definition:** A subset of [[machine-learning]] that uses **artificial neural networks inspired by the human brain** to process complex information. It is what enables face recognition on smartphones and voice assistants understanding questions → [[apercu-ia-guide]].

## What we know
- Goes "further" than plain ML for complex pattern tasks (vision, speech) → [[apercu-ia-guide]].
- The neural-net approach underpins modern generative models (LLMs behind ChatGPT, etc. — see [[prompt-chatgpt]]).

## Relations
- Subset of [[machine-learning]] ⊂ [[artificial-intelligence]].

- Source stays surface-level; no detail on architectures or training.

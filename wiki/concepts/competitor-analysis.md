# Competitor Analysis (social/marketing)

**Definition:** Studying competitors' content and performance to out-market them — on social: identify rivals, find their best/worst posts, track hashtags and brand mentions. → [[semrush-social-competitor-analysis]]

## What we know
- **3 steps:** identify biggest online rivals + best practices per network (FB/IG/Twitter/YouTube) → find top-performing content (esp. offers/promos) → discover competitor hashtags + monitor brand mentions. → [[semrush-social-competitor-analysis]]
- Tool-driven (connect own + competitor accounts/domains). → [[semrush-social-competitor-analysis]]

## Relations
- Step 2 of building a [[brand-strategy]] (market + competition analysis → positioning).
- Informs [[marketing-tactics]] and social content ([[instagram-copywriting]]); brand-mention monitoring overlaps [[online-reputation-management]] + AI visibility [[geo-tools]].
- The social counterpart of SEO competitor work (backlink gaps in [[backlinks]]).

## Tensions / open questions
- Platform data limits (historical caps); largely tool-specific.

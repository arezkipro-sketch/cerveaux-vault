# Neuroscience of Selling

**Definition:** The neurobiological framing of why persuasion works — decisions are emotional first, justified by logic after. Copy should touch the heart before the reason. → [[academie-neuroscience-vente]]

## What we know
- **Emotional brain decides first** (amygdala), prefrontal cortex justifies later → lead with emotion, then arguments. → [[academie-neuroscience-vente]]
- **Dopamine = desire engine:** anticipating a reward releases dopamine; benefit-promises, headlines and CTAs exploit the projection. → [[academie-neuroscience-vente]]
- **Trust lowers resistance:** the prefrontal cortex evaluates risk; testimonials, labels, guarantees, authentic storytelling deactivate doubt ("satisfait ou remboursé" answers a biological safety need). → [[academie-neuroscience-vente]]
- **Scarcity/FOMO** activates amygdala/insula → urgency; overuse → manipulation detected, trust collapses. → [[academie-neuroscience-vente]]

# Amazon Funnel (marketplace conversion)

**Definition:** The buyer journey on Amazon, mapped from a 1-year clickstream study — the **product page is the decisive step**. → [[semrush-amazon-funnel]]

## What we know
- ~14.35% of site visits → purchase. → [[semrush-amazon-funnel]]
- **92.4% of conversions** occur after a product-page visit (even via "Buy Now"). → [[semrush-amazon-funnel]]
- ~11.94% of sessions add to cart; ~68% of carts convert. → [[semrush-amazon-funnel]]

## Relations
- The marketplace counterpart of [[sales-funnel]]; product-page primacy reinforces [[product-bullet-points]] and product-page CRO; part of marketplace [[ecommerce|e-commerce SEO]].

## Tensions / open questions
- Browser-only data (excludes app), 2022 — may shift.

# Topic Clusters

**Definition:** A content-architecture pattern — one **pillar page** (broad overview) + interlinked **cluster pages** (sub-topics), the hub-and-spoke model. Deep coverage of a subject builds topical authority and ranks more keywords. → [[semrush-topic-clusters]]

## What we know
- **Structure:** pillar + cluster pages tied by internal links. → [[semrush-topic-clusters]]
- **Why:** more quality content on a topic → more ranking keywords + topical authority; signals E-E-A-T. → [[semrush-topic-clusters]]
- Example: pillar "decorate an apartment" + clusters kitchen/living-room/bedding.

## Relations
- The EN/Semrush equivalent of [[cocon-semantique]] (FR pages-filles → page-mère); builds [[topical-authority]] via [[maillage-interne]].
- The content-architecture layer above [[keyword-research]] and [[article-structure]].

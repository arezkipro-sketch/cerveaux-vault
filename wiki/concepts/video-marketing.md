# Video Marketing

**Definition:** The strategic use of video content to promote a brand/product/service across the buyer journey — combining visual + audio + narrative for emotional impact, message clarity, and longer attention. YouTube and TikTok act as video search engines → [[video-marketing]].

## Why
- ~90% of companies use video; **88% report positive ROI**, **80% say it boosted sales** (Wyzowl). Video raises time-on-page (SEO) and Google/YouTube favor it → [[video-marketing]].

## Video types
- Institutional, explainer, tutorial/educational, customer testimonials (case studies), product demos, short social videos, lives/webinars → [[video-marketing]].
- Map to the funnel: TOFU inspire/educate; MOFU/BOFU demos + testimonials → [[sales-funnel]].

## 6 production steps
1. [[video-script]] → 2. camera/equipment (smartphone + tripod/ring-light/mic OK) → 3. actors (authenticity) → 4. editing (cuts/subtitles/music/graphics) → 5. music + narration → 6. hosting (YouTube for SEO) + distribution → [[video-marketing]].

## Metrics
- Views + avg watch duration, retention, engagement, [[click-through-rate]], conversions, SEO/traffic → [[video-marketing]].

## Relations
- Distribution + ranking on YouTube governed by [[youtube-algorithm]]; a channel of [[social-media-strategy]] and [[search-everywhere-optimization]].
- Uses [[storytelling]], [[video-script]], ends on [[call-to-action]]; retention/CTR feed [[navboost]].

## Tensions / open questions
- Generic production advice; no per-platform specs (aspect ratio, length) or budget benchmarks.

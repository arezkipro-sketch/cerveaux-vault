# SEO Landing Pages

**Definition:** A landing page built and optimised to **rank organically *and* convert** — generating leads/sales from search without paying for ads. → [[semrush-seo-landing-pages]]

## What we know
- A conversion-focused [[landing-page]] (one action) optimised for relevant keywords so it ranks; part of inbound marketing. → [[semrush-seo-landing-pages]]
- Example: Mailchimp/Salesforce pages ranking for "email automation software". → [[semrush-seo-landing-pages]]

## Relations
- The explicit bridge between the copywriting [[landing-page]] and the SEO cluster ([[keyword-research]], [[search-intent]], [[what-is-seo|on-page SEO]]).
- Makes [[copywriting-vs-redaction-web]] concrete: one page that ranks (SEO) **and** converts (copy).

## Tensions / open questions
- Balancing keyword optimisation vs conversion focus on the same page — the central tension.

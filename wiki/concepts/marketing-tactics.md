# Marketing Tactics

**Definition:** The specific tasks that execute a marketing strategy toward a goal. "Goal = the floor up, strategy = the staircase, tactics = each step." → [[semrush-marketing-tactics]]

## What we know
- **Goal vs strategy vs tactic:** goal = outcome (more traffic/sales); strategy = approach; tactic = a concrete task (infographic, social post, email campaign). → [[semrush-marketing-tactics]]
- Tactics vary by niche, budget, company age; ~12 examples across content/social/SEO/email/paid/partnerships. → [[semrush-marketing-tactics]]

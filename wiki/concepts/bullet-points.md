# Bullet Points (copywriting)

**Definition:** More than formatting — bullets capture attention, ease scanning, and spotlight benefits to push the decision. → [[academie-bullet-points]]

## What we know
- **Why powerful:** readability, impact, clarity (complex → simple), conviction. → [[academie-bullet-points]]
- **4 types:** informative (facts) · benefit-oriented ("Économisez 2 h/jour") · intriguing (curiosity) · comparative (vs competitors). → [[academie-bullet-points]]
- **Write well:** start with a strong word or number, one short punchy line, benefits over features, vary style. → [[academie-bullet-points]]

## Relations
- E-commerce application = topic [[product-bullet-points]]; benefit bullets = FAB Benefit ([[copywriting-frameworks]]); comparative bullets use [[value-anchoring]].
- A formatting lever of [[copywriting]]; amplifies [[headline]]/[[hook]] by making benefits scannable.

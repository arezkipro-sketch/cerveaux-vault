# About Page (Page À propos)

**Definition:** Often the 2nd most-visited page after the homepage; a subtle conversion tool that builds trust and humanises the brand — written reader-first, not as an autobiography. → [[academie-page-a-propos]]

## What we know
- **5 key elements:** reader-oriented angle · authentic structured narrative (trigger → obstacles → wins → motivations) · proof/credibility (numbers, testimonials, logos, press) · values & mission · clear CTA. → [[academie-page-a-propos]]
- **6-part template:** hook/title → problem/mission → story/journey → values/philosophy → proof/results → CTA. → [[academie-page-a-propos]]
- **Memorable:** be specific, speak the audience's language, add images, update regularly. → [[academie-page-a-propos]]

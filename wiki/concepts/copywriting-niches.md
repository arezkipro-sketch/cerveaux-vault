# Copywriting Niches

**Definition:** High-demand industries a copywriter can specialise in; niching sharpens positioning and rates. AI raised demand for strategic human writers rather than killing it. → [[copyposse-profitable-niches]]

## What we know
- **13 niches:** health/wellness/medical · finance/fintech · tech/SaaS/AI · e-commerce/retail · education/edtech · B2B/enterprise · personal brands/coaching · travel/hospitality · sustainability/green/social-impact · pets · parenting/family · real-estate/local · crypto/Web3. → [[copyposse-profitable-niches]]
- **Niche down further** by sub-niche or by service (email, sales pages, VSLs, funnels). → [[copyposse-profitable-niches]]
- **Market context:** copywriting market ~$28B → $42B by 2030; "storyteller" listings doubled; trust now the top buying predictor. → [[copyposse-profitable-niches]]

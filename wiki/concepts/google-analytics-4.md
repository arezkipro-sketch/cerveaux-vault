# Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

**Definition:** Google's event-based web analytics platform. A pageview is the `page_view` event firing on each page load/reload; GA4 measures traffic, top pages and trends. → [[semrush-ga4-pageviews]]

## What we know
- **Event-based:** `page_view` fires per load/reload (repeat views counted), collected automatically. → [[semrush-ga4-pageviews]]
- **Use:** identify top-traffic pages, compare over time, find pages to improve. → [[semrush-ga4-pageviews]]

## Relations
- The measurement layer for SEO + content performance; pairs with [[google-search-console]] (search side); feeds [[seo-audit]].
- Engagement metrics (time/pageviews) support testing in [[copywriting-tools]], conversion tracking in [[sales-funnel]].
- Real-world setup example: [[ga4-setup-harnais-chien]] (installation via canal Google & YouTube Shopify, socle mesure + indexation).

## Tensions / open questions
- Pageviews vs sessions/users/engaged-sessions distinctions not deep-dived.

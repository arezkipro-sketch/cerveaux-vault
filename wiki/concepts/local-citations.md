# Local Citations

**Definition:** Online mentions of a business's NAP (name, address, phone) + website across directories. They build local search visibility and feed AI tools with correct business info. → [[semrush-local-citations]]

## What we know
- **Build via core directories:** GBP, Apple Business Connect, Bing Places, plus industry/niche directories. → [[semrush-local-citations]]
- **Why:** reach more customers, lift local SERP visibility, raise odds of AI-tool citation; drives real bookings. → [[semrush-local-citations]]
- **NAP consistency** across all listings is the underlying requirement. → [[semrush-local-citations]]

## Relations
- An off-page pillar of [[local-seo]] alongside [[google-business-profile]]; supports "near me" ranking ([[semrush-near-me-keywords]]).
- Consistent NAP → AI accuracy bridges to [[ai-seo]] / [[aeo]]; quality directories vs spam ones relate to [[toxic-backlinks]].

## Tensions / open questions
- Best niche directories vary by industry.

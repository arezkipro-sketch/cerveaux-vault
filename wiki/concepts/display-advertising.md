# Display Advertising

**Definition:** Paid banner ads shown across the Google Display Network (2M+ sites/apps) and social. Precise targeting + strong design counter "banner blindness". → [[semrush-display-ads]]

## What we know
- **What:** paid banners on sites/apps/social via the GDN; broad reach + precise audience targeting. → [[semrush-display-ads]]
- **Banner blindness:** users ignore banners (~7-32% of cases) → design is decisive. → [[semrush-display-ads]]
- 13 best practices across design/visuals, targeting, copy, CTA, testing. → [[semrush-display-ads]]

## Relations
- The visual/paid arm of [[sem]] (vs search PPC + [[negative-keywords]]); ad design + CTA reuse [[visual-copywriting]], [[call-to-action]], [[color-psychology]].
- Platform-specific paid sibling: [[pinterest-ads]] (visual, intention-based, upper-funnel) — different funnel position than interruptive display.

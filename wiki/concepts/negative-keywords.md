# Negative Keywords (PPC)

**Definition:** Words/phrases added to PPC campaigns to stop ads showing on irrelevant queries (e.g. a luxury shoe brand excludes "cheap"). → [[semrush-negative-keywords]]

## What we know
- **Benefits:** save budget (no wasted clicks), improve Quality Score (ad↔landing relevance), lift conversions, protect brand. → [[semrush-negative-keywords]]
- **Match types:** broad (default), phrase, exact — control how broadly queries are excluded. → [[semrush-negative-keywords]]

## Relations
- A paid-search (PPC) lever under [[sem]]; the paid counterpart of organic [[keyword-research]]; Quality Score ties to [[landing-page]] relevance.

## Tensions / open questions
- Google Ads-specific account vs campaign-level mechanics not exhaustive.

# Sales Page Structure

**Definition:** The strategic skeleton of a sales text — its power is the structure, not the prose. A proven 5-step order = AIDA plus a dedicated proof step. → [[academie-structurer-texte-vente]]

## What we know
- **5 steps:** (1) **Hook** — urgent problem / curiosity / specific promise. (2) **Problem** — precise diagnosis, show deep understanding → trust. (3) **Solution** — your offer as the logical answer, concrete benefit, "shortcut to the result". (4) **Proof** — testimonials, result screenshots, case studies. (5) **CTA** — clear, direct, benefit-based, urgent/limited. → [[academie-structurer-texte-vente]]
- **Pricing/value sequence:** frame value before revealing price — **valeur → preuves → mécanisme → prix** (price too early becomes the anchor). → [[academie-ancrage-mental]]
- Anchor on transformation, not features/modules. → [[academie-ancrage-mental]]
- **9 traits of top pages:** result-oriented headline (result + who + timeframe), promise backed by proof, empathetic narration ("voici VOTRE problème"), legible offer, objections handled, single repeated CTA, risk reduction. Teardowns = the fastest way to learn what/order/proof/CTA-placement. → [[academie-meilleures-pages-vente]]
- **Value proposition:** answer "why buy from you, not elsewhere?" — clear, benefit-led (feature→benefit), tied to one specific problem. → [[jeremy-allard-secrets-pages-vente]]

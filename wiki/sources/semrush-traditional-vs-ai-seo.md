# Traditional SEO vs AI SEO — Summary

**One-liner:** Semrush maps out what changes when you add AI SEO to your strategy: same foundational tasks (technical, backlinks), but keyword research becomes topic/prompt mapping and on-page optimization shifts from keyword placement to independently extractable sections. Both are needed — SEO still handles 5T Google searches/year.

## Key takeaways

### Task mapping table: Traditional SEO vs AI SEO
| Task | Traditional SEO | AI SEO equivalent |
|---|---|---|
| Research | Find queries target audience uses in Google | Discover questions target audience asks AI tools |
| On-page optimization | Optimize title tags, headings, internal links for target keywords | Write for AI extraction: direct answers, standalone sections |
| Technical SEO | Optimize crawlability, speed, mobile, indexation | Same foundations + ensure AI crawlers can access content (robots.txt, no JS-only rendering) |
| Link building | Build backlinks from relevant high-authority domains | Brand mentions across diverse sources (even unlinked) |
| Metrics | Rankings, organic traffic, CTR, conversions | AI mention share, citation frequency, brand SoV in AI |

### Prompt research vs keyword research
- Traditional keywords avg **4 words** (Petlibro example from Semrush data).
- AI prompts avg **8 words** — 2× longer; more specific, conversational, scenario-based.
- AI tools decompose queries via sub-queries ("query fan-out") → topical coverage needed → [[topical-authority]].

### On-page: standalone sections rule
- "The system extracts fragments and recombines from multiple sources → each section must be comprehensible without surrounding context."
- Bad structure: *"As mentioned earlier, several approaches should be considered…"*
- Good structure: *"Website load time depends on three factors: image optimization, server response time, code efficiency."*

### Unlinked mentions matter for AI
- Traditional SEO: backlinks = primary link equity signal.
- AI SEO: unlinked brand mentions count. Guardian article mentioning Nudie Jeans (no link) → AI Mode cites it.
- Implication: PR coverage value changes — any mention in a credible source is an AI citation opportunity.

### Tracking AI SEO performance
AI-specific metrics to track alongside traditional:
- **AI mentions**: frequency brand appears in AI responses.
- **AI citations**: frequency cited as source.
- **Share of Voice**: vs competitors across AI platforms.
- **Sentiment**: how AI tools describe the brand.

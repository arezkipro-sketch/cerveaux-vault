# Outils visuels du copywriter — Summary

**One-liner:** Canva vs Figma vs Photoshop — pick by need: Canva (fast posts/social), Figma (UI/landing mockups), Photoshop (advanced photo/ad editing).

## Key takeaways
- **Canva:** quick social posts/templates, low barrier. **Figma:** UI/landing-page mockups, collaboration. **Photoshop:** advanced photo/ad editing, steeper.
- Choose per task (posts vs landing pages vs ads); advantages + limits each.

# Exact Match Domains (EMD) — X Thread (JotaroSeo)

**One-liner:** A French X thread on Exact Match Domains — domain names matching target keywords — their current SEO value, best practices, and limits.

## Key takeaways
- **EMD definition**: a domain name that exactly matches a keyword or phrase (e.g. `taxis-paris.fr` for "taxis Paris" searches) → [[exact-match-domain]].
- EMDs are **less powerful than pre-Google Penguin** but remain useful when leveraged correctly → [[exact-match-domain]].
- Benefits: keyword signal to engines, memorability, niche targeting (especially e-commerce) → [[exact-match-domain]].
- Best practice: **max 2 keywords** in the domain; keep it short, readable, unambiguous. Use hyphens between words when multiple words are needed.
- SERP evidence: in the "taxis Paris" example, top-5 results all have EMDs containing "taxis" or "Paris" — except G7 Taxi, which ranks on brand authority and web longevity.
- **EMD is not sufficient alone** — requires quality content, strong UX, good internal linking ([[maillage-interne]]), and linkbaiting to maximize traffic.

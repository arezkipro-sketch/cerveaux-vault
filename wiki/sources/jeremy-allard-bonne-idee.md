# Savoir si une idée est bonne — Summary

**One-liner:** For founders with too many ideas who can't decide: eliminate false good ideas, validate a real market, and pick a profitable idea *without building a product first*.

## Key takeaways
- The real enemy isn't uncertainty — it's endless idea-switching and being your own brake; an imperfect decision beats perfect deliberation.
- Method: kill false good ideas, validate real market demand, choose a profitable one before creating anything.

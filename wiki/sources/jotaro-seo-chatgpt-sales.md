# ChatGPT Sales via SEO — Summary

**One-liner:** To generate sales via ChatGPT, build domain authority through [[e-e-a-t]]-driven informational content, then activate AI as a new acquisition channel alongside classic organic traffic.

## Key takeaways
- Foundation = **domain authority**: Google's (and AI's) credibility score for a site. Built through E-E-A-T content.
- **[[e-e-a-t]] definition** (4 criteria): Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness — Google's content evaluation system, now also used by AI models.
- Strategy = **informational SEO oriented toward conversion**: publish articles answering questions the buyer asks *before* searching for your product. Example: "Where to buy a pillowcase?" → leads to a pillowcase store.
- Content types that build AI-visible authority: infographics, term definitions, geolocalized articles ("Where to buy X in Paris?").
- **Off-site work**: buy backlinks to strategic pages + on-page SEO (structure, tags, content).
- **Publishing frequency**: 1 article per day or every 2 days for fastest authority growth and AI visibility.
- **Results observed** with this method: increased impressions, CTR, SERP position, AND a new AI acquisition channel unlocked.

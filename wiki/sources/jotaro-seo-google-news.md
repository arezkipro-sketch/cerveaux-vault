# Google News Ranking — Summary

**One-liner:** Google Actualités is a distinct SERP channel (evaluated on freshness, relevance, credibility) that boosts visibility and domain authority — accessed by registering in Search Console, ensuring unique URLs, and maintaining a Google News sitemap.

## Key takeaways
- **[[google-news-seo]]** definition: Google Actualités compiles articles from diverse sources, filtering by freshness, relevance, and content credibility.
- **Benefits**: boosted visibility, qualified audience, credibility signal (appearing = perceived quality), competitive advantage on trending topics.
- **Technical requirements**:
  - Register site in Search Console + submit Google News inclusion request.
  - **Unique URL per article** — no duplicate content (LA BASE).
  - **Google News sitemap** (`<news:publication>`, `<news:publication_date>`, `<news:title>`, `<news:keywords>`, `<news:genres>`) — update frequently; remove articles older than 30 days after indexation.
  - Metadata optimized.
- Impact on SEO: presence on Google News → increased traffic + quality signals → better SERP ranking.

# Analyse concurrentielle social media — Summary

**One-liner:** Beat rivals on social by studying their content — identify competitors, find their best/worst posts per network, track their hashtags and your brand mentions.

## Key takeaways
- **3 steps:** identify your biggest online rivals + their best practices (FB/IG/Twitter/YouTube); find each network's top-performing content (esp. offers/promos); discover competitor hashtags + monitor brand mentions.
- Tool-driven (Semrush Social Media Tracker): connect your accounts + competitor domains.

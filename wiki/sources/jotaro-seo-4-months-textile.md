# SEO in 4 Months — Textile Brand Case Study

**One-liner:** Case study: textile brand running on paid ads, 700€/month revenue → 2 800€/month in 4 months via a 3-pillar SEO strategy (keyword strategy + content + netlinking) preceded by a full SEO audit.

## Key takeaways
- **[[seo-audit]] is the mandatory first step** — audit before any strategy. 3 audit types:
  - **Internal audit**: content SEO-friendly? anchor texts optimized? internal linking quality? on-page tags?
  - **External audit**: competitive SEO maturity, current site positions, relative domain authority vs. competitors (Semrush).
  - **Technical audit**: fix 404 errors, 301 redirect optimization, Core Web Vitals, crawlability.
- **3-pillar SEO strategy**: (1) keyword strategy (long-tail focus), (2) content strategy (informational articles), (3) netlinking (backlinks to strategic pages).
- Brand already had social media presence but posted rarely — multi-channel brand signal used as SEO leverage.

# Publications GBP — Summary

**One-liner:** GBP posts are free quick updates (offers, events, product highlights) that appear in Google Search + Maps, boosting local SEO and foot traffic.

## Key takeaways
- **What:** short updates on your Google Business Profile — offers, upcoming events, product highlights — shown in Search + Maps when people find your business.
- **Why:** keep info current, build loyalty, improve local SEO, drive in-store traffic.

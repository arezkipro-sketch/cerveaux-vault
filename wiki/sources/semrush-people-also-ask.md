# People Also Ask (PAA) — Summary

**One-liner:** Google's "People Also Ask" box shows related questions on the SERP, each answered from a specific page — appearing there = extra visibility + a keyword-idea mine.

## Key takeaways
- **What:** PAA shows questions related to the query, often near the SERP top; clicking reveals a short answer (paragraph/list/table/image/video) pulled from a page.
- **Why:** appearing in both classic results + PAA boosts visibility; PAA is also a source of new question keywords to target.
- **Find PAA questions:** SERP inspection, keyword tools, autocomplete.

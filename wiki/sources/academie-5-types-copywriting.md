# Les 5 types de copywriting — Summary

**One-liner:** Copywriting splits into 5 approaches, each tied to one objective; a real copywriter switches between them by goal.

## Key takeaways
- **5 types:** (1) **Direct response** — push immediate action (sales pages, emails, ads); measurable in conversions. (2) **SEO copywriting** — content optimised for search + readers; long-term organic traffic. (3) **Branding** — build identity/notoriety, not immediate sale (slogans, ad storytelling). (4) **Technical / B2B** — vulgarise complex info to convince rational deciders (case studies, technical spec sheets); persuasion by logic + credibility. (5) **Emotional** — play on feelings/desires, strong in B2C, drives impulse buys.
- **Pick by objective:** sell fast → direct; attract traffic → SEO; build brand → branding; convince B2B → technical; emotional connection → emotional.
- A pro juggles all five — that's the line between a plain writer and a copywriter.

## Notable claims (with locations)
- "Le copywriting direct… pousser le lecteur à agir immédiatement." — *(§1)*
- "Le copywriting émotionnel… particulièrement efficace en B2C pour créer une connexion immédiate et pousser à l'achat impulsif." — *(§5)*

# Calendriers de contenu — Summary

**One-liner:** A content calendar plans and visualises what/when/where/who across blog, social and email over weeks/months.

## Key takeaways
- **Tracks:** what content, when + where it publishes, who's responsible, links to assets.
- **Built in:** spreadsheets (Excel/Sheets), calendar apps, or specialised tools (CoSchedule); 4 example layouts provided.

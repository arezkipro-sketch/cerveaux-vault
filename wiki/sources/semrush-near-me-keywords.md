# Mots-clés « près de moi » — Summary

**One-liner:** "Near me" searches surged (~+400% YoY 2019-21); win them with an optimised GBP, local terms, schema, title tags and internal links.

## Key takeaways
- **What:** "[service] près de moi" queries signal intent to find a local business by current location. Google ranks by **proximity, relevance, business info**.
- **Optimise:** complete/verified [[google-business-profile|GBP]] (hours, categories), local keywords in site + listings, schema markup, title-tag optimisation, internal linking.
- A full GBP with all key info (e.g. Sunday hours) wins relevant local searches.

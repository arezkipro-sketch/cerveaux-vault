# Citations locales — Summary

**One-liner:** A local citation = an online mention of your business's NAP (name, address, phone) + site; builds local visibility and feeds AI tools like ChatGPT with correct info.

## Key takeaways
- **What:** online mentions with NAP info (e.g. Yelp). Build via core directories: GBP, Apple Business Connect, Bing Places, industry/niche directories.
- **Why:** reach more customers, boost local SERP visibility, increase odds of being cited by AI tools — drives real bookings (dental clinic on ADA/Zocdoc/Healthgrades example).
- **NAP consistency** across listings is the underlying requirement.

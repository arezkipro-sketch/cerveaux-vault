# SEO off-page — Summary

**One-liner:** Off-page SEO = improving rankings via signals *outside* your site (links, mentions, reviews) to build trust/authority; on-page + off-page + technical are the three pillars.

## Key takeaways
- **Definition:** efforts to rank higher without changing the site itself — link building, guest posts, social. Goal: make search engines + users see the site as trustworthy and authoritative (E-E-A-T).
- **On vs off vs technical:** on-page = your pages; off-page = outside reputation; technical = site-wide speed/security/mobile.
- **5 working tactics:** link building (#1), + (rest of the 5 in the body) brand building, content marketing, digital PR, local/reviews.
- **Authority data (Semrush 2024 Ranking Factors):** domain Authority Score = 6th strongest predictor of ranking position; then page authority score, referring IPs (domain), referring domains (domain), referring IPs (URL), referring domains (URL).

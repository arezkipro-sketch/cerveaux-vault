# 12 tactiques de marketing — Summary

**One-liner:** Tactics = the specific tasks that execute a strategy toward a goal — "goal = the floor up, strategy = the staircase, tactics = each step".

## Key takeaways
- **Goal vs strategy vs tactic:** goal = desired outcome (more traffic/sales); strategy = the approach; tactic = a specific task (publish an infographic, post on social).
- Tactics vary by niche, budget, company age; 12 examples span content, social, SEO, email, paid, partnerships, etc.

# Que et quand publier (étude social) — Summary

**One-liner:** A 1-year study of top tech brands on IG/Twitter/FB/YouTube: **content type matters more than day/time** for engagement.

## Key takeaways
- The content types that get most engagement aren't the ones brands post most.
- **Content type > timing** for engagement; analysed together, day/time has no significant impact.
- Most popular posting window = midweek (Tue-Thu) 9am-3pm — but popularity ≠ engagement.
- Caveat: tech brands, 2022 data (pre-Threads); may not generalise to other sectors.

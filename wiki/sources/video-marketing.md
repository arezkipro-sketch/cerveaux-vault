# Vidéo marketing — Summary

**One-liner:** Video marketing is the strategic use of video across the buyer journey — combining visual + audio + narrative for emotional impact — now used by ~90% of companies; this guide covers why, video types, the 6 production steps, and the metrics.

## Key takeaways
- **Definition:** strategic video to inform/educate/entertain/persuade, integrated into the wider digital strategy. YouTube + TikTok act as **video search engines**.
- **Why invest** (Wyzowl): ~90% of companies use video; **88% report positive ROI**, **80% say video boosted sales**. Video pages raise time-on-page (helps SEO); Google/YouTube favor video.
- **Video types:** institutional (brand story/values), explainer, tutorial/educational, customer testimonials (case studies), product demos, short social videos, lives/webinars.

## Strategy + 6 production steps (with locations)
- **Strategy:** define objective (educate/leads/authority) → know audience → map to the **customer journey** (TOFU inspire/educate; MOFU/BOFU demos/testimonials) → budget/time *(§stratégie)*.
- **6 steps** *(§6 étapes)*: 1. **script** (structure/pace/message), 2. camera/equipment (smartphone + tripod/ring-light/mic OK), 3. actors (authenticity; CEO > external actor for institutional), 4. editing (cuts/subtitles/music/graphics; pacing per format), 5. music + narration, 6. **hosting** (YouTube for SEO; Vimeo/social/own site) + distribution plan.

## Metrics (with locations)
- Views (+ avg watch duration), **retention**, engagement rate, **CTR**, conversions, SEO/traffic — via YouTube Analytics, Google Analytics, social stats *(§mesurer)*.

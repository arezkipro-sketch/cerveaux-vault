# Neurosciences & vente — Summary

**One-liner:** Decisions are emotional first, justified by logic after; copy should touch the heart before the reason — a neurobiological framing of the existing persuasion levers.

## Key takeaways
- **Emotional brain (amygdala) decides first**, prefrontal cortex justifies later → touch emotion before arguments.
- **Dopamine = desire engine:** anticipating a reward releases dopamine; benefit-promises/CTAs/headlines exploit this ("Obtenez votre premier client en 30 jours" = positive projection).
- **Trust lowers resistance:** prefrontal cortex evaluates risk; testimonials, labels, guarantees, authentic storytelling deactivate doubt. "Satisfait ou remboursé" neutralises the biological need for safety.
- **Scarcity/FOMO:** rarity activates amygdala/insula → urgency; but overuse → brain detects manipulation, trust collapses. Use authentically.

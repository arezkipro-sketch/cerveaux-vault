# AEO vs SEO — Summary

**One-liner:** Semrush defines AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) as the discipline of getting content cited in AI-generated responses, distinct from SEO (ranking in traditional SERP) — they share foundations but have different success metrics and surface types → [[aeo]].

## Key takeaways

### AEO definition
- **AEO = Answer Engine Optimization**: optimizing for content to appear in or be cited by AI-generated responses (ChatGPT, Google AI Mode, AI Overviews, voice search).
- 77 % of ChatGPT US users treat it as a search engine. ~60 % of consumers use AI tools for shopping research.
- AEO extends SEO; doesn't replace it → [[ai-seo]].

### SEO vs AEO comparison
| Dimension | SEO | AEO |
|---|---|---|
| Goal | Rank in traditional SERP + AI features | Be cited/mentioned in AI-generated answers |
| User behavior | Browse results, choose to click | Read AI answer, may explore cited sources |
| Discovery surface | SERP links, snippets, PAA, AI Overviews, AI Mode | AI responses, citations, brand mentions |
| Success metric | Ranking, CTR, organic traffic | Mention frequency, citation share, brand visibility in AI |

### How they work together
- Strong SEO builds domain authority and content trustworthiness → feeds AEO.
- Authoritative domain + specific extractable content = best outcome for both.
- Google AI Mode sidebar: lower overlap with traditional top-10 than its "additional links" (which overlap more).
- Perplexity has highest overlap with traditional Google. ChatGPT has lowest.

### What drives AEO specifically
- Original data and first-hand experience → cited over generic content.
- Named authors with credentials → credibility signal for AI.
- Community mentions (Reddit, Wikipedia, forums) → AI trust signal → [[semrush-ai-visibility-study-findings]].
- Brand mentions across diverse sources → builds "cooccurrence" in AI training data → [[ai-seo]].

## Connections / contradictions
- AEO ≈ GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) — same concept, different term. [[aeo]] page will disambiguate.
- Extends [[ai-seo]]: AEO is the Semrush brand name for the citation-optimization layer.
- Pairs with [[semrush-google-ai-mode]] for understanding where AEO surfaces.
- The "community content > brand content" finding from [[semrush-ai-visibility-study-findings]] is the surprise: you can do both SEO and AEO and STILL not be cited if you're not validated on community platforms.

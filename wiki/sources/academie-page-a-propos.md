# Page À propos convaincante — Summary

**One-liner:** The About page is often the 2nd most-visited page; it's a subtle conversion tool — reader-oriented, not an autobiography.

## Key takeaways
- **5 key elements:** reader-oriented angle (why should your story interest the client?) · authentic structured narrative (trigger → obstacles → wins → motivations) · proof/credibility (numbers, testimonials, logos, press) · values & mission · clear CTA.
- **6-part template:** hook/title → problem/mission → story/journey → values/philosophy → proof/results → CTA.
- **Memorable:** be specific (not "je suis passionné"), speak the audience's language, add images, update regularly.

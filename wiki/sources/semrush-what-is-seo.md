# Qu'est-ce que le SEO — Summary

**One-liner:** SEO = making a site more visible in search engines without paying for ads, to earn organic rankings, brand awareness and traffic.

## Key takeaways
- **Definition:** process of earning free/organic visibility in Google etc.; aka organic/SEO marketing.
- **4 pillars of a full SEO strategy:** keyword research (what the audience searches) · on-page SEO (pages + content) · off-page SEO (links/reputation) · technical SEO (performance + accessibility).
- **SEO vs SEM:** SEM promotes via paid + organic; SEO is the organic side.

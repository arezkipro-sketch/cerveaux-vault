# AI Replacing Writers — Summary

**One-liner:** AI didn't kill writing — it *exposed* it, drawing a hard line between commodity writers (dying) and strategic writers (thriving, best-paid ever).

## Key takeaways
- **Two types of writers AI separated:** (1) generic/templated/interchangeable = **commodity writing** (AI replaces this) (2) strategic writers who understand positioning, psychology and brand — more valuable, in-demand, better-paid than ever.
- Some content "writing" jobs are genuinely dying; denying it is dishonest. But the strategic category AI "will never touch" is growing.

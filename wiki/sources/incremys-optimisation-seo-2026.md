# Optimisation SEO 2026 — Summary

**One-liner:** Data-driven 2026 SEO: measurable visibility → business results, across fragmented journeys (SERP, rich results, AI answers) where zero-click is rising (~60%, Semrush 2025) and source trust differentiates.

## Key takeaways
- **4 mutually-reinforcing levers:** technical/crawl (HTTP, robots, sitemap, canonicals, JS) · content/relevance (intent, structure, coverage) · authority (quality links, mentions, brand consistency) · measurement/iteration (prioritise → deploy → validate → fix).
- 2026 shift: beyond "position" — optimise for AI-assisted answers + zero-click; trust is a differentiator.

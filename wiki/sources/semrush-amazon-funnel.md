# Amazon Funnel Insights — Summary

**One-liner:** A 1-year Amazon clickstream study (Dec 2021–Nov 2022) maps the buyer funnel — and the product page is decisive.

## Key takeaways
- **14.35%** of site visits end in a purchase.
- **92.4%** of conversions happen after visiting a product page (even via "Buy Now") → the product page is the pivotal asset.
- ~11.94% of sessions include an add-to-cart; of carts, ~68% convert (8.12% of sessions).

# Autorité en copywriting — Summary

**One-liner:** Winners aren't the best writers but the ones who inspire most trust; authority lowers perceived risk → faster sales + higher rates.

## Key takeaways
- **Authority = 4 signals:** clarity (I understand what you do) + proof (you've done it) + process (how you work) + consistency (same quality everywhere).
- **Why it matters:** reduces risk (decide faster), raises rates at equal skill (higher perceived value), attracts good clients/repels bad.
- **6 pillars:** clear positioning (by channel/market/format/problem) · demonstration not assertion (teardowns, before/after) · decision-oriented portfolio (context/objective/approach/proof/excerpt) · qualitative social proof (before/during/after + detail) · "proof content" not chatter · network.
- **5 killers:** publishing without a through-line, illegible offer, perfectionism before acting, copying others, not showing work.
- **14-day plan:** positioning+offer → MVP portfolio (3 pieces) → 4 demonstrative contents → outreach + conversations.

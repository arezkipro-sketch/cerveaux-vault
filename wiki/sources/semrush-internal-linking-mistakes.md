# Erreurs de liens internes — Summary

**One-liner:** Internal links are signposts that help Google understand site structure and distribute page authority; common errors (broken links, orphan pages) undermine SEO + UX.

## Key takeaways
- **Why fix:** internal links show Google the site structure → crawl/index + authority distribution; errors confuse Google → pages uncrawled/unindexed.
- **11 common errors include:** broken internal links, orphan pages (no inbound links), too-deep pages, non-descriptive/over-optimised anchor text, nofollow on internal links, redirect chains, etc.

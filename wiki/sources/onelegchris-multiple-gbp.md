# Two GBP Profiles = 60 %+ Clicks — Summary

**One-liner:** A tactical local SEO tip: deploy two overlapping Google Business Profile listings so they both appear in the same map pack, capturing position 1 (36 % clicks) + position 2 (24 % clicks) = 60 %+ before any competitor clicks in → [[google-business-profile]].

## Key takeaways
- Single GBP listing covers ~4-5 mile radius → [[google-business-profile]].
- Place second GBP where radius overlaps slightly with first.
- Both appear in same search → together take position 1 + 2 in map pack.
- Position 1 click share: **36 %**, position 2: **24 %** → together **60 %+** of clicks before competitors appear → [[local-seo]].
- "Two billboards in one search" — author's framing.

## Caveats / risks
- Author does not mention Google ToS explicitly. Multiple GBPs for the same business are technically permitted only if they represent distinct physical locations or distinct service-area businesses.
- Risk: if Google detects artificial duplication (same address, same brand), listings may be suspended.
- Safest use: genuine multi-location business or separate service entities operating from different addresses.

# Copywriting Instagram — Summary

**One-liner:** The photo stops the scroll, the words convert; treat each caption as a short sales text, mobile-first, engagement-driven.

## Key takeaways
- **What's unique:** short/dynamic (first lines before "voir plus"), emotion-seeking audience, algorithm rewards engagement (likes/comments/saves/shares).
- **Caption structure:** punchy hook (first line before the cut) → story/value → natural transition → clear CTA → 3-5 relevant hashtags.
- **7 tips:** clarity, storytelling, ask questions (drive comments), emojis sparingly, line breaks/bullets, brand-consistent tone, test & analyse.
- **Mistakes:** just describing the photo, too many/generic hashtags (#love #instagood), text walls, no CTA, ignoring the caption.

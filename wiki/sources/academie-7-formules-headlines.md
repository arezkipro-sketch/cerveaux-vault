# 7 formules de headlines — Summary

**One-liner:** A headline is your "first sale" — the sale of attention; 7 plug-and-play formulas + a 15-min write-10-keep-3-test method.

## Key takeaways
- **Why headlines convert:** a prospect *scans*, doesn't read; the headline is a brutal filter. It must (1) grab attention, (2) promise clear value ("what do I gain — now?"), (3) push to continue (curiosity/desire/contrast/soft urgency).
- **3 ingredients of a converting headline:** a Result (what they want) + a Mechanism (how/why possible) + a Proof/risk-reduction. E.g. "Augmenter vos conversions **sans augmenter votre trafic**".
- **7 formulas:** (1) "Qui d'autre veut [result]?" (belonging+curiosity) (2) "La vérité sur [problem]" (revelation+authority) (3) "Comment [result] en [delay] même si [objection]" (4) "[N] façons de [result]" (lists reassure) (5) "Le moyen le plus simple de [result] sans [sacrifice]" (low friction) (6) "Avertissement: ne faites pas [error] avant…" (prevention) (7) "Maintenant vous pouvez [result] grâce à [new method]" (opportunity).
- **Method (15 min):** define audience in one sentence → pick a dominant emotion → write 10 titles → keep best 3 by filter (specific, credible, reader-oriented "vous", jargon-free).
- **Test on:** click rate, scroll/read time, final conversion, micro-actions.
- **Mistakes:** vague, impossible promises, excessive mystery, title disconnected from content, jargon.

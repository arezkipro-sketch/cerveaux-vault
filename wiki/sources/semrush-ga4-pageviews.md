# Pages vues GA4 — Summary

**One-liner:** In GA4, a pageview is an event (`page_view`) firing each time a page loads/reloads; it shows which pages get the most traffic and how traffic trends.

## Key takeaways
- **GA4 is event-based:** `page_view` fires on every load/reload (repeat views counted), collected automatically.
- **Use:** find top-traffic pages, compare traffic over time, spot pages to improve.

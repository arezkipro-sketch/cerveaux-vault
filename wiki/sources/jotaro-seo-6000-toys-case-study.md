# 100€ → 6000€ in 3 Months — Toys E-Commerce Case Study

**One-liner:** Toy e-commerce case study: a trending product with low competition + rapid SEO execution = 60× revenue growth in 3 months, using Google Trends + Semrush + SERP analysis + competitive benchmark.

## Key takeaways
- **Trending product first**: identify a viral/trending product on social media, then validate search demand via Google Trends before investing in SEO.
- **[[keyword-research]] process**: (1) Google Trends for trend validation, (2) Semrush/Ubersuggest for volume + long-tail discovery, (3) SERP analysis to understand competitor landscape.
- **SERP analysis / benchmark**: only one niche competitor + Amazon in top 3. Low competitive resistance = strong opportunity signal.

# Apprendre le copywriting sans expérience — Summary

**One-liner:** You can learn copywriting from zero — master fundamentals, study performers, practise daily, get feedback; land first clients via prospecting + samples.

## Key takeaways
- **4 steps:** understand fundamentals (persuasion, sales-text structure, headline, storytelling) → study what worked (ads/sales pages/emails) → practise daily (titles, emails, short pages, get feedback) → get accompaniment.
- **Beginner errors:** writing "like an author" instead of for conversion, neglecting audience research, copying without understanding, delaying practice from fear.
- **First clients fast:** direct LinkedIn/email prospecting, professional communities, showcase samples (even fictional ones).

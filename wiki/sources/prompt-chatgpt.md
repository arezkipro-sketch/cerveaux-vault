# Le Pouvoir du Prompt ChatGPT — Summary

**One-liner:** The quality of a ChatGPT answer is determined by the prompt — clarity, specificity, and context turn generic replies into strategic, actionable solutions; mastering prompting is a strategic skill driving digital transformation and AI ROI.

## Key takeaways
- **A prompt = the instruction/question given to the AI.** Prompt quality directly determines answer quality — vague prompt → vague answer ("garbage in, garbage out").
- **The three levers:** **clarity, specificity, context.** Add audience, objective, tone, scenario, format, length.
- **General vs detailed:** "Stratégies de marketing digital" → broad/superficial; "3 on-page SEO techniques a startup can apply to a tech blog to grow organic traffic in 6 months" → strategic, actionable, tailored to audience/goal/timeframe.
- **Role prompting:** "Imagine you are a digital-marketing consultant…" yields sector-adapted, more relevant answers.
- **Productivity:** well-formed prompts reduce iterations, save time, extract deeper insights; for content pros they produce richer, personalized content fit for "answer-friendly" ([[aeo]]) demand.
- **Digital transformation:** prompt-fed ChatGPT automates repetitive tasks, generates strategic insight, improves customer service, supports decisions; campaigns in minutes; startups prototype/simulate/assess risk cheaply. **Prompt quality = the ROI of AI adoption.**

## Notable claims (with locations)
- **70% of ChatGPT queries are personal use** (tutoring, translation, brainstorming, summarizing) — ITPro/OpenAI *(§Qu'est-ce qu'un prompt)*.
- Answer accuracy improves with specific/structured prompts — ResearchGate student-prompt study *(§Qu'est-ce qu'un prompt)*.

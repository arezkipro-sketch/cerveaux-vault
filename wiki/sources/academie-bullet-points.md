# Bullet points en copywriting — Summary

**One-liner:** More than formatting — bullets capture attention, ease reading, and spotlight benefits to drive the buy.

## Key takeaways
- **Why powerful:** readability (airy, scannable), impact (highlight essentials), clarity (complex → simple), conviction (guide to the decision).
- **4 types:** informative (facts/features), benefit-oriented ("Économisez 2 h/jour"), intriguing (curiosity: "le secret méconnu…"), comparative ("contrairement aux autres formations, 1 client en 30 jours").
- **Write well:** start each with a strong word or number, one short punchy line, benefits over features, vary the style.
- **Example:** turn a flat paragraph into 3 benefit bullets (Apprenez plus vite / Obtenez vos premiers clients en 30 jours / Augmentez vos revenus).

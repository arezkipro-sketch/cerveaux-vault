# 3-Month AI Visibility Index Trends — Summary

**One-liner:** Semrush AI Visibility Index, 3 months (Aug–Oct), tracking ChatGPT and Google AI Mode brand mentions and source diversity across verticals: ChatGPT is highly volatile (source diversity near-doubled in one month), Google AI Mode is more controlled, top 50 brands are stable, bottom half of top 100 fluctuates.

## Key takeaways

### Volatility: ChatGPT vs Google AI Mode
- ChatGPT brand mentions: **+12 % new brands mentioned in September**, then revert in October → highly volatile → [[ai-seo]].
- Google AI Mode brand mentions: **-4 %** across Aug–Oct → more controlled, tighter recommendation set.
- ChatGPT sources: **+80 % source diversity increase in October** — near-doubling of sources cited per category.
- Google AI Mode sources: +13 % Aug–Oct → much more stable.
- **Key insight**: AI search can change fundamentally in one month. Track each platform independently → [[ai-seo]].

### Top brand stability
- **Top 50 brands in AI visibility are relatively stable** (±20 % weighted Share of Voice).
- 25 new brands entered top 100 in 3 months; most from lower positions.
- **61 % of top 100 brands are common to ChatGPT and Google AI Mode** → high-authority brands recognized across platforms.
- Long-term brand building still matters — the top 50 don't move dramatically.

### Weighted Share of Voice (pondération)
- Metric: **80 % weight to ChatGPT + 20 % to Google AI Mode** = combined AI visibility score → [[ai-seo]].
- Accounts for usage share (ChatGPT dominates AI traffic at 85 %+) → [[semrush-ai-referral-traffic]].

### Vertical behavior (ChatGPT, Aug–Oct)
- Consumer electronics: **+20 % unique brands mentioned**.
- Technology & Software: **+12 %**.
- Finance (YMYL): **-15 % brand diversity** → AI becoming more conservative on financial recommendations.

### Notable events detected
- Possible ChatGPT algorithm change in September (brand diversity spike then revert).
- Google removing "num=100" parameter may have affected AI search source pools.
- Reddit still dominant source for ChatGPT across all non-YMYL verticals.

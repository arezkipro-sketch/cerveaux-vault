# Meilleurs Outils d'AI Visibility en 2026 — Summary

**One-liner:** A comparison of the top 5 AI-Visibility tools (Ubersuggest, Profound, Semrush, Ahrefs, ScrunchAI) that track brand mentions/citations inside LLM answers — the "AI-era" SEO instruments needed because traditional analytics can't measure zero-click AI exposure.

## Key takeaways
- **AI Visibility tools = AI-era SEO tools:** they monitor brand mentions and content citations across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Google AI Overviews, Bing AI. They solve a measurement gap traditional analytics miss.
- **Why now:** Gartner predicts **traditional search volume down 25% by 2026** (AI/virtual assistants). The **zero-click** effect means users get answers without visiting sites — so where/how often your brand appears in AI answers matters as much as Google rank.
- **Combine** AI Visibility data with classic metrics (views/positions/backlinks) for the full picture; don't replace one with the other.
- **Common metrics:** Brand Visibility (mention count), Industry Rank, Top Prompts, Competitor Visibility, Sentiment, Authority (by topic cluster), a single visibility/SoV score.

## The top 5 (with positioning)
- **[[ubersuggest]]** — AI Visibility inside its SEO suite; flat monthly price + **unlimited projects** (agency-friendly); stable data; metrics: Brand Visibility / Industry Rank / Top Prompts / Competitor Visibility. *(NP Digital's own tool — listed first; promotional bias.)*
- **[[profound]]** — enterprise; deep context: **Sentiment, competitive mentions, Authority**; custom dashboards + alerts; high annual pricing, not for small business.
- **[[semrush]]** — AI Visibility added to its suite; visibility **score** (like domain authority); connects AI citations to backlinks/rankings; agency pricing limits projects.
- **[[ahrefs]]** — backlink leader; AI Visibility filters by engine + competitor compare; standout = **links↔AI-citation correlation** (backlinked content gets cited more → reveals what makes content "citable"); premium price, project limits.
- **[[scrunchai]]** — newcomer, **AI-Visibility-only**; tracks ChatGPT/Claude/Perplexity/Google AIO/Bing AI + emerging; real-time alerts; analyzes **quality + frequency** (primary/secondary resource, misinfo detection); **query simulation**; priced by query volume not projects; young-company risk.

## Choosing criteria (with locations)
- Budget; team technical level; dedicated vs integrated; scalability (per-project limits vs unlimited); data reliability (mature infra vs new tools); update cadence/roadmap *(§Choisir le bon outil)*.

# Structurer un texte de vente en 5 étapes — Summary

**One-liner:** A sales text's power is its structure: a 5-step skeleton (hook → problem → solution → proof → CTA) — essentially AIDA + proof.

## Key takeaways
1. **Hook** — irresistible opener: urgent problem, curiosity, clear specific promise.
2. **Problem** — precise diagnosis; show you understand the prospect better than anyone → emotional connection + trust.
3. **Solution** — present your offer as the logical/natural answer; be specific on the concrete benefit; position as a shortcut to the result.
4. **Proof** — testimonials, result screenshots, case studies/data → credibility, lowers objections.
5. **CTA** — clear, direct, benefit-based, visible, urgent/limited.

# Search Everywhere Optimization — Summary

**One-liner:** Search is fragmented — people search on YouTube, TikTok, Pinterest, ChatGPT, Amazon, podcasts, not just Google — so the "new SEO" is optimizing a brand's organic presence **across every platform the audience searches**, an approach NP Digital is actively building.

## Key takeaways
- **Search Everywhere Optimization = SEO across all platforms.** Benefits: reach/notoriety, engagement, conversions, **algorithm-change resilience**, and a larger SEO surface (Google indexes video/podcasts/social).
- **5 platform categories:** (1) traditional search, (2) social search, (3) AI search, (4) e-commerce, (5) "underdog" (podcasts, voice, forums).
- **Cross-platform constant:** strengthen **E-E-A-T** everywhere + consistent brand identity. Platform-specific tactic example: keywords in bios.
- **Resource-limited?** Start with the platforms your customers actually use.

## Platform tactics (with locations)
- **Traditional (Google 90%+, ~⅔ web traffic; Bing for B2B):** backlinks, technical, deep content; for AI Overviews add expert citations + E-E-A-T *(§Recherche traditionnelle)*.
- **YouTube (#2 search engine):** keyword titles, detailed descriptions, full transcripts; target uncovered intents (AnswerThePublic) → [[youtube-algorithm]].
- **Social search:** avg user 7 platforms/month; ~40% of young users prefer IG/TikTok over Google. Fundamentals: consistent profile names, complete profiles, keywords in bios/posts, quality visuals, trend content.
  - **Instagram/Facebook:** awareness, engagement, product discovery; keywords/hashtags/geo, creator collabs, visual consistency.
  - **TikTok:** commercial discovery engine; influences Google search; trending hashtags, viral formats, influencers.
  - **Pinterest:** visual search engine (retail/decor/fashion/lifestyle); pin titles/descriptions, board names, alt tags, bio keywords.
- **AI search:** ChatGPT builds notoriety even without direct traffic. **GEO/LLM optimization:** brand image, expert structured content, FAQ per article, site structure, **schema markup**, perfect indexation — "easy to read for humans AND machines."
- **Underdog:** brand **podcasts** (less saturated; podcast SEO + DPR), **voice search** ("write as people speak", long-tail, structured data), **forums** (Reddit + Quora; 100+ searches/sec contain "reddit") → [[reddit-seo]].
- **Shopping:** Google Shopping + product schema; **Amazon** (keyword titles, reviews, A+ content); **App Stores** (ASO).

## Implementation (5 steps)
1. Identify audience search habits (GA, surveys). 2. Adapt content per platform (same message, different formats). 3. Data-driven (TikTok Insights, IG Analytics, YouTube Studio, GSC). 4. Optimize local + voice (Google Maps, Yelp, NAP, FAQ). 5. Consistent brand identity.

## Challenges
- Limited resources (prioritize), changing algorithms, personalization-vs-consistency, internal silos (cross-team collaboration).

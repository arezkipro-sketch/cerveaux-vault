# 7 erreurs de débutant — Summary

**One-liner:** The 7 beginner traps that slow copywriting progress — mostly writing for self over reader, skipping audience research, and no CTA.

## Key takeaways
- **7 mistakes:** (1) trying to learn everything at once (2) neglecting audience research (3) copying without understanding (4) writing for yourself not the reader ("faire agir" > "bien écrire") (5) forgetting the CTA (6) not practising regularly (7) self-teaching with no feedback.

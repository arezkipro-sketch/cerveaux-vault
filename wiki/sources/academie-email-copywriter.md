# Email Copywriter — Summary

**One-liner:** A specialised, high-demand role writing persuasive sales emails; €30-70k/yr; email ROI ~$36 per $1 — recipients give ~9 seconds.

## Key takeaways
- **Role:** writes commercial emails where each word moves to one action; distinct from informational web writing (conversion-focused). Formats: sales sequences, welcome/onboarding, newsletters, win-back, nurturing.
- **Skills:** buying psychology (pains/desires/objections), the art of the subject line (~30% of emails read <2s), action-oriented structure (AIDA/PAS), conversational tone ("write like a friend").
- **Income (FR freelance, Glassdoor):** beginner €20-30k (€50-150/email), intermediate €30-50k (€150-350), expert €50-80k+ (€350-1000+); some bill on results (% of sales).
- **Context stats:** ~9s read time (Litmus); email ROI ~$36/$1.

# Google Official AI Overviews Ranking Guide — Summary

**One-liner:** @irentdumpsters (Bodhi, local SEO practitioner) summarizes Google's own documentation on how to rank in AI Overviews — key signal: AI optimization IS classic SEO; experience-first content; no scaled content abuse; GBP and media matter.

## Key takeaways
- **AI optimization IS SEO**: Google's AI Overviews use the same core systems as traditional Google Search. No separate AI-only playbook → [[ai-seo]].
- **Write from experience**: not generic "best of" lists. Personal/expert experience is what AI favors → [[e-e-a-t]].
- **"Scaled content abuse"**: new Google term for mass-producing near-identical content targeting keyword variations. Penalized → [[e-e-a-t]].
- **Stay indexed**: basic prerequisite — page must be crawlable and indexed before AI can cite it → [[crawl-budget]], [[canonical-tag]].
- **Add photos and video**: media signals matter for AI Overview citations → [[ai-overviews]].
- **Use Google Business Profile**: GBP products and business info can surface directly in AI responses for local queries → [[google-business-profile]], [[local-seo]].
- **Browser agents / AI agents**: future relevance — AI agents are beginning to read DOM structure and accessibility trees, not just rendered text. Site accessibility = future AI citability factor.

# Storytelling — Summary

**One-liner:** Storytelling = persuasion that puts the *prospect* at the centre of the story; emotion + identification beat rational argument.

## Key takeaways
- **Why it works:** stories trigger dopamine/oxytocin → proximity (reader projects in), memorisation (mental images), engagement, faster action.
- **4 pillars:** (1) an identifiable hero = your prospect (2) a central conflict/pain (3) a narrative arc (initial situation → problem → quest → resolution) (4) a convincing resolution where your offer becomes obvious.
- **Techniques:** engage the senses, play on contrasts (before/after, pain/relief), include dialogue, use metaphors.
- **Mistakes:** stories too long, disconnected from the offer, useless detail, no emotional link to the prospect.
- **Storytelling × SEO:** raises read time, shares, conversion — positive ranking signals when paired with structured optimised content.

# Effet de rareté — Summary

**One-liner:** Limited = valuable (FOMO); 3 forms of scarcity, applied transparently or it backfires and kills trust.

## Key takeaways
- **Psychology:** scarcity ties to FOMO; brain equates rare = precious. Universal driver.
- **3 forms:** quantity (limited seats/stock), time (clear deadline), access (VIP/private circle).
- **Apply ethically:** justify the limit ("50 élèves max pour un suivi de qualité"), positive framing ("10 premiers = bonus") not threats, stay consistent (a "limited" offer that always returns = discredited).
- **Concrete levers:** countdowns, limited webinar/live seats, early-bird bonuses.
- **Trust:** be transparent on real limits, avoid fake urgency, always tie scarcity to a clear buyer benefit.

# Comment créer une newsletter ? Guide pratique — Summary

**One-liner:** A newsletter is an *owned*, recurring editorial channel (not algorithm-dependent like social) that converts strangers → leads → clients → ambassadors — built on a quality opt-in list, GDPR-compliant consent, automation, and segmentation.

## Key takeaways
- **Treat the newsletter as an editorial product** (format, editorial line, clear objective), not "promotional emails." Its edge over social: **control, predictability, and preference** — you own the audience, free of algorithm swings.
- **Newsletter ≠ email marketing.** Email marketing = punctual, direct sales/promo, short-term conversion ("a salesman knocking"). Newsletter = recurring, editorial, long-term value even with no offer ("a weekly consultant"). Top firms use **both, complementarily**.
- **List building: quality > quantity.** Give a real reason to subscribe (exclusive content, discounts, sector reports, event invites). Tactics: site forms/landing pages/popups, **lead magnets** (ebooks/checklists/whitepapers), webinars/events, social integration.
- **Ethical collection only.** Buying lists violates GDPR and yields low-quality, disengaged contacts. Collect voluntarily + transparently.
- **GDPR consent is mandatory:** clear **opt-in**, **double opt-in** (confirmation email), and easy unsubscribe — also a trust/authority lever.
- **Automation + segmentation are the pillars:** welcome sequences, behavior-triggered flows (e.g. ebook download → topic flow), 60-day-inactive re-engagement. Segment by profile / behavior / funnel stage — "don't send pepperoni pizza to vegetarians."

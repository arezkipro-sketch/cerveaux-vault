# Écrire des emails qui vendent — Summary

**One-liner:** Email enters the prospect's personal space with no algorithm filter; a 4-step method writes a selling email in <30 min.

## Key takeaways
- **Why email:** direct channel, no algorithm; well-written = real connection + action.
- **Subject + opener:** subject decides open ("Comment doubler vos rendez-vous en 30 jours" >> "Newsletter de septembre"); first line = a handshake (question, stat, short story).
- **Body = benefits not features:** "Vous gérez vos campagnes 2× plus vite" >> "interface intuitive". Concise, rhythmic, one idea per paragraph; interest → desire.
- **Proof:** specific testimonials/numbers placed strategically, not overloaded.
- **CTA:** clear, benefit-worded, repeated (esp. at end).
- **4-step method:** identify pain/desire → present solution → add concrete proof → strong unambiguous CTA.

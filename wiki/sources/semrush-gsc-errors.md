# Erreurs GSC — Summary

**One-liner:** Google Search Console monitors/manages your site's presence in Google and surfaces crawl/index errors; unresolved errors block indexing and hurt rankings.

## Key takeaways
- **GSC** = free Google service to monitor search presence + flag crawl errors Googlebot hits.
- **Why fix:** unresolved errors → pages not indexed (won't appear in results) + bad UX (a ranking factor).
- Common errors: 404s, redirect errors, indexing/coverage issues.

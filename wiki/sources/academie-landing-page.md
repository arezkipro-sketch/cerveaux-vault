# Landing page qui convertit — Summary

**One-liner:** Unlike a normal web page, a landing page has ONE goal — conversion — built on a fixed structure.

## Key takeaways
- **Essential elements:** catchy title (states the promise) · clear subtitle (offer + added value) · impactful visual (photo/video/mockup) · social proof (testimonials, client logos, verified numbers) · visible CTA repeated at strategic spots.
- **Title:** speak to the reader, concrete benefit, answer a precise problem ("Obtenez votre premier client en 30 jours…").
- **CTA:** visible, contrasted, benefit-worded ("Réservez votre appel gratuit" not "Envoyer").
- **Mistakes:** too much info, weak CTA, no proof, non-mobile-optimised design.
- **Advanced levers:** scarcity, authority, personalisation by traffic source, storytelling.

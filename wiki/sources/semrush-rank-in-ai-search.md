# Rank in AI Search: 6 Expert Tactics — Summary

**One-liner:** Semrush's 6-tactic playbook for AI search visibility: monitor brand narrative first (not just traffic), then build brand authority via PR + data studies, structure content for extraction, update regularly, answer customer questions, use video as citation magnet. Key stat: 90% of AI-cited content updated in last 3 years; only 6% older than 6 years.

## Key takeaways

### Tactic 1: Track AI presence (3 layers)
- **Layer 1 — Brand narrative**: What are AI tools saying about you? SoV vs competitors + sentiment score. Alex Birkett (Omniscient Digital): *"I want to check if my brand is described accurately and consistently. Sentiment is not the only goal."*
- **Layer 2 — Direct traffic**: Check GA4 AI referral sources + server logs for bots like `ChatGPT-User`, `PerplexityBot`. Traffic won't be conclusive but shows if AI can access your site.
- **Layer 3 — Business impact**: Track conversions from AI referral + ask on sales calls "how did you find us?" — brands report significant portion of leads come via ChatGPT mentions.

### Tactic 2: Build brand authority where it counts
- Brand search volume = strongest predictor of AI chatbot brand mentions (Kevin Indig research).
- Tactics: guest posts on reputable publications, publish data-driven studies others cite, own branded community (HubSpot subreddit: 14,000 members → top AI citations for CRM/marketing queries).
- Not just being known — being cited for the right reasons in the right contexts → [[citation-absorption]].

### Tactic 3: Structure content for AI extraction
- H2/H3 as questions → AI recognizes Q&A format.
- Answer at the top of each section → information appears quickly.
- Comparison tables → directly match AI Mode's exploratory use case → [[semrush-google-ai-mode]].

### Tactic 4: Update content regularly
- **90 % of AI-cited content updated within last 3 years** (Semrush data).
- Only **6 %** of AI-cited content older than 6 years.
- Updates: replace outdated stats, add new examples, fix broken links, update screenshots, reoptimize structure.

### Tactic 5: Create content answering real customer questions
- Prompt research: find queries where brand is absent vs competitors (Semrush AI Visibility Toolkit).
- Sources: sales call transcripts, support tickets, chat logs, GSC branded keyword data.
- Example: Chargeblast identified gap prompts → created targeted pages → closed visibility gaps.

### Tactic 6: Use video as citation magnet
- YouTube videos appear in ChatGPT and Google AI Overviews for informational queries.
- Best formats: how-to tutorials, visual demos, side-by-side comparisons ("Mailchimp vs ActiveCampaign for small business").

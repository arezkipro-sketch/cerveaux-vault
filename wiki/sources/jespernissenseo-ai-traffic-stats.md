# AI Traffic Platform Stats — Summary

**One-liner:** SE Ranking study on AI-referred traffic: ChatGPT dominates with 78 %+ share; AI platforms collectively deliver only 0.33 % of global web traffic — but that share doubled in the past year and converts at unusually high rates → [[ai-seo]].

## Key takeaways (source: SE Ranking study)
- **ChatGPT**: **78.22 %** of all AI-referred web traffic → [[ai-seo]].
- **Perplexity**: 9.33 %.
- **Gemini**: 6.85 %.
- Combined AI traffic = **0.33 % of global web traffic** → currently negligible at macro level.
- But: that 0.33 % doubled in one year → growth trajectory significant → [[ai-seo]].
- AI-referred visitors reportedly convert at higher rate than traditional search — anecdotal from multiple practitioners, not yet confirmed at scale.

## Strategic implications
- **ChatGPT first**: for AI visibility strategy, ChatGPT citation optimization = highest leverage. Aligns with [[alexgroberman-citation-absorption]] finding that ChatGPT has the highest per-source influence score (0.2713).
- **0.33 % doesn't mean ignore**: early adopters building AI citation base now will compound as AI traffic grows.
- Volume gap vs. conversion advantage: fewer AI visits but each one warmer/higher-intent.

- Validates [[wiki/sources/jotaro-seo-ia-x-seo]] ChatGPT focus (10M+ monthly active users claim; 78 % traffic share confirms dominance).

- Reinforces [[ai-seo]] as a long-play investment (0.33 % now, 2× YoY growth).

# FRW Ep. 280: Making Herstory — Summary

**One-liner:** Crossover episode where Nicki and Kate share their origin stories in copywriting and the founding of CCA, on a student's podcast Herstory.

## Key takeaways
- CCA grew from a "crash course" Nicki developed for junior writers she hired at a travel company; launched in 2012
- Business scaled to primary income from 2018 onward
- Copywriting is a "word puzzle" blending creativity with strategy — not purely artistic
- The biggest hurdle for students: battling internal limiting beliefs ("you can't do this"), not the craft itself
- The only way to fail in copywriting is to stop showing up; momentum builds slowly then cascades
- CCA offers lifetime access and ongoing course updates (e.g., LinkedIn training refresh)

## Notable claims
- "The only way to 'fail' is to quit." — Nicki *(episode)*
- Kate: English teacher background → found freedom through copywriting after finding the podcast
- Nicki still periodically takes on clients to stay sharp and bring fresh examples to students *(episode)*

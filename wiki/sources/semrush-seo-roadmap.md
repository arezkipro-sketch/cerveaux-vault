# Feuille de route SEO — Summary

**One-liner:** An SEO roadmap is a document formalising a step-by-step plan (tasks, timelines, owners) to improve search performance toward business goals.

## Key takeaways
- **What:** a custom step-by-step plan with tasks, deadlines, assignees (built in a sheet/PM tool); e.g. a 9-month roadmap.
- **Why:** simplifies complex SEO into manageable tasks ("keyword research → optimise pages" vs vague "improve SEO"); prioritises high-impact efforts; aligns team + timelines.

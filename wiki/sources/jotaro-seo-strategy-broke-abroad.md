# SEO Strategy Building — Broke and Abroad Case Study

**One-liner:** Full walkthrough of how to analyze a site and build an SEO strategy using the travel agency Broke and Abroad as example — covers SEO audit, market analysis, and keyword strategy with SEMrush.

## Key takeaways
- **[[seo-audit]] + market analysis** precede any strategy. The audit covers: brand identity, site structure, content quality, internal link architecture.
- **Thematic blog** with tag-organized content = good structure (Broke and Abroad).
- **Underexploited sites**: last article from August 2024 despite competitors (Tripadvisor) publishing daily = warning sign.
- **Keyword research process**: (1) type primary keyword in Semrush, (2) find related questions, (3) each question = one article target.
- **Time-sensitive content**: travel/news sites need content aligned with seasonal demand — can't spend too long on low-demand content.

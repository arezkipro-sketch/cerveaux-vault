# Publicités display — Summary

**One-liner:** Display ads are paid banners across the Google Display Network (2M+ sites/apps); precise targeting + good design beat "banner blindness".

## Key takeaways
- **What:** paid banner ads on sites/apps/social via the GDN; reach + precise audience targeting.
- **Banner blindness:** users ignore banners (~7-32% of cases, 2023 study) → design matters to get noticed/clicked.
- 13 best practices across design/visuals, targeting, copy, CTA, testing.

# Rédiger un lead magnet irrésistible — Summary

**One-liner:** A lead magnet (guide/checklist/mini-course/template) is a free resource traded for an email; irresistible = solves one urgent problem and leads to the next funnel step.

## Key takeaways
- **3 steps:** (1) identify the audience's urgent problem (blockers, repeated mistakes, the quick win they want) (2) craft a clear desirable promise (catchy title + specific benefit + measurable result + simple method/delay) (3) structure & write valuable content (brief intro w/ credibility → step/point body using AIDA/PAS/FAB + bullets → resources/templates → logical CTA to the next step).
- **Promise examples:** "Checklist: 10 étapes pour structurer votre première page de vente"; avoid vague "Guide du copywriting".
- **Promote:** embed in top blog posts (internal links), pop-up/banner, LinkedIn/newsletter, webinar bonus.

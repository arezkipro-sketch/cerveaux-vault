# Mots-clés négatifs — Summary

**One-liner:** Negative keywords stop PPC ads from showing on irrelevant queries (e.g. a luxury shoe seller excludes "cheap") — saving budget and sharpening targeting.

## Key takeaways
- **What:** words/phrases added to PPC campaigns to prevent ads triggering on irrelevant searches.
- **Benefits:** save money (no wasted clicks), improve Quality Score (more relevant ads↔landing pages), increase conversions, protect brand.
- **Match types:** broad (default), phrase, exact — control how broadly queries are excluded.

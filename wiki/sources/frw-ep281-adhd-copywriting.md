# FRW Ep. 281: Copywriting Success with ADHD — Summary

**One-liner:** CCA student Caleb shares tactical systems for turning ADHD into a competitive copywriting advantage, including sprint methods and environment design.

## Key takeaways
- ADHD advantage: hyperfocus during research phase enables finding unique angles others miss
- Sprint method: work intensely for short bursts then full break to avoid mid-day fog
- Key principle: can't rely on "feeling" like working — must set up environment so starting is the easiest option
- External accountability (creative team collaboration) is especially valuable for neurodivergent copywriters
- Journalism background (interviewing skills) transfers directly to copywriting research
- CCA content can be consumed in chunks as needed rather than sequentially

## Notable claims
- "Consistency looks different for different people, different seasons of your life." — Caleb *(episode)*
- "Cool thing about copywriting is you could switch up your arrangement just however it suits you." — Caleb *(episode)*

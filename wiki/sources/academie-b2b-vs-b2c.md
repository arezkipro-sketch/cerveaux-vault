# Copywriting B2B vs B2C — Summary

**One-liner:** Same goal (convert), different levers — B2B = rational/ROI/long collective cycle; B2C = emotional/immediate/short individual cycle.

## Key takeaways
- **B2B:** professional, rational tone; argue value/ROI/reliability; long detailed content (case studies, white papers); long collective decision cycle. Technique: figures + case studies + educational emails.
- **B2C:** direct, empathetic, emotional tone; concrete immediate benefits; short impactful copy (emails, ads, sales pages); fast individual purchase. Technique: catchy titles, testimonials, scarcity/urgency.
- **Adapt:** B2B → data, proof, experience; B2C → emotion, benefits, transformation; both → hook early + guide to action.

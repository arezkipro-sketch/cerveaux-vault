# Comment écrire un bon hook — Summary

**One-liner:** A hook is the first impression that earns the reader's "keep going"; build it from the audience's main desire/pain + an emotion.

## Key takeaways
- **What:** a hook ("hameçon") is a punchy phrase/concept grabbing attention immediately; forms = intriguing question, shocking stat, captivating story, strong promise.
- **4 traits of a good hook:** clarity (instantly understood), impact (strong emotion: surprise/curiosity/fear/desire), relevance (tied to the offer), originality (not generic).
- **4 hook types w/ examples:** question ("Et si vous pouviez doubler vos ventes…?"), statistic ("80% des visiteurs quittent après 3 secondes…"), narrative ("…un email qui a généré 6 000 € en 24h…"), promise ("Dans 5 minutes, vous allez découvrir…").
- **3-step method:** identify audience's main desire/pain → craft a phrase stirring emotion around it → ensure it leads naturally into the sales message.
- **Mistakes:** too vague, lying/exaggerating (destroys trust), ignoring the audience.

# Psychological Triggers (discovery calls) — Summary

**One-liner:** You lose clients on discovery calls because of *energy*, not portfolio or pricing — shift from "pick-me" to confident leadership and clients chase you.

## Key takeaways
- **Core shift:** the best (high-paying, low-micromanage) clients hire confidence, not desperation. Don't wait to feel expert to act like a boss.
- **The call is about leading the client, not getting the client.** Not "fake it" — lead with calm authority even without all the answers (no one has them).
- Promises 5 confident one-liners that shift the power dynamic on sales/discovery calls.

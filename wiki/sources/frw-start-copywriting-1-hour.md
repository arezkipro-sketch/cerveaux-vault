# Start Copywriting 1 Hour a Day — Summary

**One-liner:** Alex Cattoni's 5-phase, 10-week blueprint for building a real copywriting career in one focused hour per day around a busy life.

## Key takeaways
- Phase 1 (Wk 1–2): Study + practice writing daily. Study days (Mon/Wed/Fri): 20 min tutorial + 40 min practice. Writing days (Tue/Thu): 60 min pure writing. Goal: 3 pieces you're proud of.
- Phase 2 (Wk 3–5): Build portfolio. You don't need clients — use mock copy. Portfolio must be curated (not a random dump), showing 3–5 strategic assets for one offer/brand.
- Phase 3 (Wk 5–7): Outreach. 20 min/day find 3 prospects → 30 min write/send 2 personalized applications → 10 min follow up. ~40 applications/month; you only need one yes.
- Phase 4 (Wk 7–10): Overdeliver on first client. Ask for testimonial + referral. Same effort to close $500 vs $5,000 client — upsell.
- Phase 5 (Mo 3+): Build systems: Discovery System, Client Attraction System, Onboarding System.
- Realistic income timeline: Mo 3 = $500–1K, Mo 4 = $2–3K, Mo 6 = $5K, Mo 12 = $10K.

## Notable claims
- "One focused hour beats three distracted hours every single time." *(article)*
- "It takes the same amount of effort to close a $500 client as a $5,000 client." *(article)*
- Warm outreach and referrals outperform cold outreach (cold avg. 1–5% response rate) *(article)*
- Student: £600 client turned into £6,000 client by offering full marketing funnel *(article)*

# Mots-clés de question — Summary

**One-liner:** Question keywords are queries starting with who/what/where/when/why/how etc.; rising with voice search — target them to win visibility, traffic and niche authority.

## Key takeaways
- **What:** queries with a question indicator (qui/quoi/où/quand/pourquoi/comment…). Most-searched globally: "que regarder".
- **Why:** growing with voice search + Google's specific-answer push; build authority + relevant traffic.
- **Find via:** Keyword Magic "Questions" filter, online communities, Google autocomplete, "People Also Ask".

# 35 Ways to Find Copywriting Clients — Summary

**One-liner:** Don't rely on one prospecting method (the client-search trap) — try many; the more you get out there, the more clients you find; referrals make it easier over time.

## Key takeaways
- **Core principle:** beginners pick one search method and stick to it → spotty results + frustration. Spread across many tactics, give each a solid effort.
- **Online tactics (sample):** contact previous colleagues (fastest growth — they vouch for you), run webinars, careful FB/Google/LinkedIn ads, branded FB page with valuable info, Quora/Medium value posts (no hard sell), networking groups, LinkedIn profile, portfolio newsletter + opt-in bonus, host online events for decision-makers, free 15-min *what-not-how* copy evaluations.
- **Free consults rule:** give the *what* (weak benefit, unclear CTA spotted), not the *how* (the writing they hire you for).

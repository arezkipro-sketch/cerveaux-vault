# Mots-clés organiques — Summary

**One-liner:** Organic keywords drive *free* search traffic (vs paid PPC keywords); optimise for them to rank and earn traffic without ad spend.

## Key takeaways
- **Definition:** keywords you rank for organically → free traffic + conversions, unlike PPC terms advertisers bid on.
- **Find them via:** keyword tools (e.g. Keyword Magic, 24B+ keyword DB), competitor analysis, GSC, "people also ask"/related searches.
- Use them to target rankings aligned with intent.

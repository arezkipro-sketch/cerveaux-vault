# HVAC Local SEO Case Study — Summary

**One-liner:** HVAC contractor invested 27 k$ in local SEO over 12 months and grew from near-zero to 3.2 M$ revenue — driven by high-intent emergency pages, internal linking, GBP optimization, and real local backlinks → [[local-seo]].

## Key takeaways
- **27 k$ investment → 3.2 M$ revenue** in 12 months (HVAC contractor, US local market) → [[local-seo]].
- **Strategy pillars**:
  1. **High-intent money pages**: emergency repair pages (e.g. "AC repair [city] same-day") + service + location combinations. Targets customers in active distress = highest conversion intent → [[search-intent]].
  2. **Internal linking**: pages interlinked by service + location for crawlability and PageRank distribution → [[maillage-interne]].
  3. **GBP optimization**: fully built-out Google Business Profile as local signal → [[google-business-profile]].
  4. **Local content**: city/neighborhood-specific content to build relevance for service areas → [[local-seo]].
  5. **Real backlinks**: local news, directories, sponsors — not PBN. Domain authority lift → [[seo-audit]].
- Emergency service keyword targeting = highest commercial intent in local service industries → [[search-intent]].

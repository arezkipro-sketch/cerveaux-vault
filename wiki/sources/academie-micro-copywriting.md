# Micro-copywriting — Summary

**One-liner:** Microcopy = the tiny interface texts (buttons, field labels, error messages, hints) — often the last friction point before conversion.

## Key takeaways
- **Why each word matters:** reduces hesitation (clear field labels), guides action (CTA), builds trust (human error messages), reinforces brand voice.
- **Examples:** buttons ("Essayer gratuitement", "Recevoir le guide"); field hints ("Nous ne partagerons jamais votre adresse"); errors ("Oups, cette adresse semble invalide. Vérifiez la syntaxe" > "Erreur"); onboarding steps; ethical scarcity ("Plus que 3 places").
- **6 principles:** clarity first, concision (1-2 lines), action (verbs), empathy (answer the user's fear at that moment), consistency (brand tone), test & optimise.
- **Mistakes:** vague ("Soumettre" < "Envoyer ma demande"), pointless jargon, ignoring context, overloading.

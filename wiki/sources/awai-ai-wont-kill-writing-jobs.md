# AWAI Blog — AI & Writing Jobs Summary

**One-liner:** AWAI's blog index page featuring articles on AI tools for copywriters, freelance career paths, habit building, and writing success stories.

## Key takeaways
- AI should handle execution tasks (research, drafting, brainstorming) while humans retain strategy, judgment, big-idea finding, and message shaping
- The fastest way to get better AI output: give it 3–5 great examples so it learns the pattern (Rebecca Matter's approach)
- "Habit stacking" and "skill stacking" build stronger neural pathways for productive writing routines
- Writing niches unaffected by AI: case studies, ghostwriting, B2B values-driven messaging
- There are 7 weekend freelance writing projects that can pay $1,000 or more

## Notable claims
- Copywriters should use AI for execution, reserve strategy and the "big idea" for themselves — *(AWAI blog)*
- Ghostwriting is broader than books for celebrities — 13 unexpected ways writers ghost for others — *(blog listing)*
- B2B copywriter Stacy Eleczko blends purpose-driven values with AI strategies without sacrificing quality — *(success story)*

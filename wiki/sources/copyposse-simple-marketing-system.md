**One-liner:** Most copywriters struggle to get clients due to *marketing*, not skill — a simple repeatable system beats posting/outreach more.

## Key takeaways
- The bottleneck is a missing marketing system, not writing ability.
- A simple, repeatable system (clear offer + consistent visibility + nurtured pipeline) turns beginners into booked copywriters — work the system, not more volume.

# SEO landing pages — Summary

**One-liner:** An SEO landing page is built and optimised to rank organically *and* convert — generating leads/sales without paying for ads.

## Key takeaways
- **Definition:** a landing page (one action: signup/trial/purchase) optimised to rank for relevant keywords → leads/sales from organic. E.g. Mailchimp/Salesforce ranking for "email automation software".
- Part of inbound marketing; combines conversion-focused [[landing-page]] design with on-page SEO + keyword targeting.

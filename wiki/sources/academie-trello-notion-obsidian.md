# Trello vs Notion vs Obsidian — Summary

**One-liner:** Three organisation tools for copywriters/creators — pick by working style: Trello (visual kanban), Notion (flexible database/workspace), Obsidian (interconnected notes).

## Key takeaways
- **Trello:** kanban lists/cards — task pipelines, editorial-calendar tracking, easy client collaboration; ultra-simple, many integrations.
- **Notion:** flexible all-in-one workspace/database — docs, wikis, databases; powerful but steeper.
- **Obsidian:** interconnected markdown notes (this vault's tool) — networked knowledge, backlinks, local files.

# Intention de mots clés — Summary

**One-liner:** Keyword/search intent = the reason behind a query; Google ranks pages that best match it, so aligning content to intent is essential.

## Key takeaways
- **4 intent types:** informational ("que sont les graines de chia"), navigational, commercial (researching before buying), transactional ("acheter des graines de chia").
- **Why it matters:** Google shows results matching intent (product carousel for "acheter…", articles for "que sont…"). Mismatched intent = won't rank.
- Identify intent via SERP analysis or a keyword tool's intent column.

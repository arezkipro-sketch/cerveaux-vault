# AI SEO Traffic Study — Summary

**One-liner:** Semrush study (500+ marketing/SEO topics across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Google AI Mode) projects AI search visitors to surpass traditional organic visitors by early 2028 — and LLM visitors already convert at 4.4× the rate of traditional organic visitors.

## Key takeaways

### Traffic trajectory
- AI search traffic projected to **surpass traditional organic search** by early **2028** → [[ai-seo]].
- If Google AI Mode becomes the default experience, this transition could happen much sooner.
- LLM visitor count grew 8× between Oct 2023 and Apr 2025 (ChatGPT weekly active users reference) → [[ai-seo]].
- Short-term: combined total traffic (traditional + AI) will initially decrease, then stabilize, then slowly increase (funnel compression effect).

### Conversion quality
- **LLM visitor value = 4.4× traditional organic visitor** (based on conversion rate comparison) → [[ai-seo]], [[citation-absorption]].
- Reason: by the time an LLM visitor clicks, they've already consumed a synthesized answer comparing options — they arrive further down the funnel.
- AI recommendations feel like "word-of-mouth" → higher emotional impact and persuasion than blue-link clicks.

### Why AI traffic compresses the funnel
- AI search provides informational content at the point of query — reduces need to visit multiple sites.
- Links in AI responses are less visible/prominent than traditional SERP links.
- "De-prioritizes links" — some citations are not clickable at all (brand mentions without URLs).

### Strategic implication
- Small AI traffic share at current volumes can already generate equivalent economic value to traditional search if conversion rate premium holds.
- Estimated: AI and traditional search will generate similar economic value by end of 2027 → [[ai-seo]].

## Connections / contradictions
- Extends [[jespernissenseo-ai-traffic-stats]]: AI = 0.33% of global web traffic NOW, but this study says it surpasses traditional by 2028 — consistent trajectory.
- Reinforces [[alexgroberman-citation-absorption]]: if LLM visitors convert 4.4× better, then absorption > selection is even more important (you want the AI to recommend, not just mention you).
- "Funnel compression" = new risk not in existing wiki: AI may eliminate mid-funnel content entirely.

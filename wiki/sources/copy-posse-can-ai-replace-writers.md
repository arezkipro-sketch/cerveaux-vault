# Can AI Replace Writers? — Summary

**One-liner:** Real business owners, copywriters, and CMOs explain why they still hire human writers over AI, backed by Goldman Sachs and Anthropic data.

## Key takeaways
- 68% of small businesses already use AI (Goldman Sachs), but 80% say it enhances rather than replaces their workforce
- Nearly 40% of small businesses using AI are creating new roles because of it
- Anthropic's Economic Index (4M+ conversations) shows majority of AI use augments workers, not replaces them
- 50%+ of long-form LinkedIn content is now AI-written (Originality.AI) — pendulum is swinging back to human voice
- Brands are actively labeling "no AI" content to address growing consumer skepticism
- Competitive advantage of human copywriters: quality control, strategy, ownership of results

## Notable claims
- "The freelance writing market has been split in two. The bottom is collapsing. The top is thriving." — MediaBistro *(article body)*
- "AI writes fast but it never learned what to leave OUT." — LinkedIn commenter *(article body)*
- Neil Patel: "AI doesn't create originality. It creates the statistical average of the Internet." *(article body)*
- Companies that ditched copywriters for AI are experiencing "detrimental consequences" and old clients are returning — ABC News *(article body)*

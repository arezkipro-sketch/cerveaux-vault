# 7 prompts ChatGPT page de vente — Summary

**One-liner:** 7 prompts that walk ChatGPT through a sales page step by step — each maps to a sales-page section.

## Key takeaways
- **7 prompts:** (1) list audience pains (2) emotional + concrete benefits (3) 5 hooks (4) detailed page structure (5) social-proof angles (6) 5 CTAs (7) top 10 objections + rebuttals.
- **Best use:** adapt each prompt to product/audience, combine AI output with your expertise/style, test formulations. AI doesn't replace copywriting mastery.

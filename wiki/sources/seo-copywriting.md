# SEO Copywriting — Summary

**One-liner:** SEO copywriting writes for both people *and* search engines — content that ranks AND persuades to action — distinct from content writing (which informs/builds trust); covers the on-page elements, an 8-step best-practice workflow, the 4 search intents, and how to become an SEO copywriter.

## Key takeaways
- **SEO copywriting = ranking + persuasion.** Not keyword stuffing; write for humans first, optimized so engines also understand.
- **SEO copywriting vs content writing** (table): copywriting → conversion+visibility, strategic SEO, CTAs essential, metrics = CTR/conversion/ROI, **direct** business impact. Content writing → inform/educate, variable SEO, CTAs optional, metrics = time/shares, **indirect**. "Content writing builds trust; SEO copywriting turns trust into measurable results."
- **Ranking/conversion elements:** site speed (<2s), title tag, strong content, meta description, keyword frequency, page links.

## 8 best practices (with locations)
1. **Generate content ideas** — Ubersuggest "Content Ideas"; AI (ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini) then validate in an SEO tool *(§1)*.
2. **Turn ideas into keywords** — Ubersuggest "Keyword Ideas"; Google Trends, AnswerThePublic, Exploding Topics *(§2)*.
3. **Optimize title / meta / H1** — title 50-60 chars w/ keyword early; meta <160 chars (not a ranking factor but drives CTR); one H1, 20-70 chars *(§3)*.
4. **Create complete content** — quality, freshness, solve one problem; **AI as collaborator not replacement** (Austin Cosler, NP Digital: expertise/originality/emotion are the rules) *(§4)*.
5. **Keyword frequency + placement** — frequency vs density; avoid stuffing; place naturally *(§5)*.
6. **Internal links w/ optimized anchor** — structure + UX *(§6)*.
7. **Link to high-quality external pages** — credibility + reciprocal backlinks *(§7)*.
8. **Rich media** — images/infographics/video for readability + engagement *(§8)*.

## Search intent (4 types)
- Informational, navigational, **commercial** (compare before deciding), transactional. Align message + structure + CTA with the dominant intent *(§Search Intent)*.

## Notable claims (with locations)

# SEM — Summary

**One-liner:** Search Engine Marketing = boosting search visibility via *both* paid ads ("sponsored") and unpaid/organic (SEO) results; both keyword- and intent-driven.

## Key takeaways
- **SEM = paid + organic:** paid search shows "sponsored" results (PPC); SEO earns unlabeled organic results. Using both maximises visibility/traffic/leads/revenue.
- **Keyword-driven:** the right keywords in the right places power both ads and content; success depends on matching **search intent** (why people search).

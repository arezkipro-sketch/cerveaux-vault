# Améliorer ses textes avec ChatGPT — Summary

**One-liner:** Use ChatGPT as a copywriting assistant — speed, idea variation, optimisation — but keep strategy, originality and human review.

## Key takeaways
- **Why:** save time (titles/hooks/bullets in seconds), vary formulations, get inspiration, optimise (spot long/unclear sentences).
- **Best uses:** title ideas; benefit bullets (informative/intriguing/comparative); restructure with AIDA/PAS/FAB; **simulate the client's voice** (needs/objections); generate A/B variants.
- **Limits:** doesn't replace strategy (needs precise guidance), originality stays key (support not substitute), human verification mandatory for coherence/authenticity.

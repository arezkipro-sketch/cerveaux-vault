# 26 AI SEO Statistics 2026 — Summary

**One-liner:** Semrush compilation of 26 AI SEO statistics covering adoption, AI Overviews, traditional SEO impact, content, and tools — with sources. Key projections: AI traffic exceeds traditional search by 2028; ~60 % of searches produce zero clicks.

## Key stats by category

### Adoption / growth
- **ChatGPT**: 700 million weekly active users (4th most visited site globally, 5B+ monthly visits). *(OpenAI, 2025)*
- **Google AI Mode**: 100 million users in US and India. *(Google, 2025)*
- **AI search traffic +527 % YoY** (Previsible AI Traffic Report 2025, 19 GA4 properties, Jan–May 2024 vs 2025: ~17k → 107k sessions).
- Some sites now report **>1 % of total sessions from AI platforms** (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Copilot).
- AI traffic projected to **surpass traditional search by 2028** → [[semrush-ai-seo-traffic-study]].
- Gen Z / young millennials adopting AI search fastest; some countries' low-income adoption **4× higher** than high-income.

### AI Overviews reach
- Google AI Overviews: **2 billion monthly users, 200+ countries** → [[ai-overviews]].
- 99.9 % informational intent queries covered (consistent with [[wiki/sources/jotaro-seo-ia-x-seo]]).

### Zero-click problem
- **~60 % of searches now produce zero clicks** (search intent satisfied directly in SERP/AI response without visiting a site) → [[ai-overviews]], [[ai-seo]].

### AI SEO ROI
- **~70 % of companies report higher ROI from using AI in SEO** (vs. not using AI tools).
- **~35 % of Gen Z in the US use AI chatbots for search**.

### LLM value
- LLM visitor value = **4.4× traditional organic visitor** (conversion rate basis) → [[semrush-ai-seo-traffic-study]].
- AI traffic combined value will equal traditional search value by end of 2027 (projected).

## Connections
- These stats reinforce and quantify [[ai-seo]] and [[ai-overviews]] claims throughout wiki.
- "60 % zero-click" is the sharpest framing of why traditional rank alone is insufficient.
- Google AI Overviews at 2B users / 200 countries = now mainstream (not experimental).
- 527 % YoY growth rate = the best single stat for making the case to invest in AI SEO now.

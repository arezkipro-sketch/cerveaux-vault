# Google AI Mode — Summary

**One-liner:** Google AI Mode is a distinct search experience (separate tab, conversational, multimodal, agentic reasoning) that is fundamentally different from AI Overviews — it uses Gemini's advanced model with sub-query synthesis and offers richer formatting, while serving exploratory rather than quick-answer queries → [[google-ai-mode]].

## Key takeaways

### What AI Mode is
- Google AI Mode is a **separate tab** in Google Search (alongside Images, Videos), also accessible at google.com/aimode.
- Available via Search Labs opt-in; being gradually expanded.
- Powered by **advanced Gemini model with agentic reinforcement learning** (not the same Gemini as AI Overviews).
- Now at **100 million users** in US and India → [[semrush-ai-stats-2026]].

### AI Mode vs AI Overviews — comparison table
| Feature | AI Overviews | AI Mode |
|---|---|---|
| Access | Appears in standard search results when useful | Separate tab or google.com/aimode |
| Purpose | Quick summaries for complex questions | Deep interactive exploration, comparisons, planning |
| Interaction | Static response with support links | Conversational — follow-up questions, context retained |
| Best for | Rapidly understanding complex topics | Exploratory tasks, product comparisons, tutorials, planning |
| Input types | Text only | Multimodal: text, voice, images |
| AI model | Custom Gemini embedded in Search systems | Advanced Gemini with agentic RL for enhanced reasoning |
| Response depth | Summary of key info + useful links | Synthesized from multiple sub-queries + diverse sources |
| Visual experience | Primarily text | Rich visuals, actionable links (booking, tutorials, etc.) |
| Trigger frequency | Selective — only when highly confident | Broader — applies to complex queries with quality thresholds |

### How AI Mode differs from other AI tools
- Unlike ChatGPT or Perplexity (standalone AI), AI Mode operates within Google's ranking ecosystem → SEO still matters.
- Perplexity has the most overlap with Google traditional results; ChatGPT has the least → [[semrush-aeo-vs-seo]].
- AI Mode sidebar has LOWER overlap with traditional top-10 organic vs. "additional links" section.

### Optimization for AI Mode
- Foundational: same as traditional SEO + AI SEO (crawlability, indexation, authority, E-E-A-T).
- AI Mode specifically: structured content supporting exploratory multi-step questions.
- Comparison content (tables, pros/cons) → directly matches AI Mode's use case for exploratory tasks.
- Sub-query coverage: 40-60 pages per category → [[topical-authority]] still applies.

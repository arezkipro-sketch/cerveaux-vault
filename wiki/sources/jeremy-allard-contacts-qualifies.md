# Trouver des contacts qualifiés — Summary

**One-liner:** A survival method to find qualified leads when launching with no audience, no ads, no network — and you can't afford to waste effort on false prospects.

## Key takeaways
- Targets the high-stakes solo/reconversion moment: avoid the "fog with a broken compass" of busy-work that doesn't produce leads.
- Method = qualify hard (avoid false prospects), focus effort on genuinely reachable, fit prospects — secure leads deliberately rather than spray-and-pray.

# Semantic Opportunities — Case Study

**One-liner:** How identifying "semantic opportunities" — adjacent keyword targets that address the same buyer need with different vocabulary — multiplied client revenue from 2-3k to 5 figures/month.

## Key takeaways
- **Market analysis first**: understand persona, know the product's exact purpose — before any keyword research.
- **SERP analysis**: analyze the SERP for the primary keyword to surface what Google actually returns and what it reveals about buyer intent.
- **Semantic opportunities**: keywords that share the same underlying buyer need but use different vocabulary. Example: retrogaming console buyer also searches "cadeau geek original", "comment jouer aux jeux de son enfance", "émulateur vs console retrogaming".

# 15 techniques d'écriture persuasive — Summary

**One-liner:** Persuasion is a learnable structured craft, not a gift — 15 techniques to influence a decision without forcing or manipulating; "écrire plus juste, pas plus".

## Key takeaways
- **15 techniques:** (1) promise a clear benefit up front (2) back claims with precise, verifiable facts/citations (3) plant pre-agreement ("when you finish, you'll agree") (4) match persuasion level to the reader's existing agreement (5) simplify arguments (analogies/metaphors) (6) lead to "yes" by small successive steps (7) use proven frameworks (AIDA…) (8) choose the right tone (9) write to one individual (10) establish authority with concrete details (11) raise & reverse objections early (12) write in the audience's language (13) use high-impact words ("dream" > "goal") (14) loop back and reinforce earlier points (15) close strong with an explicit CTA.
- **FAQ clarifies:** persuasive writing = broader (articles, emails, speeches); **copywriting = its sales-oriented application**. It's a skill not a talent; not manipulation if intent is clear and honest.

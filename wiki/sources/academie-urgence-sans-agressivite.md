# Urgence sans agressivité — Summary

**One-liner:** Urgency works (FOMO) but heavy-handed = aggressive and credibility-killing; trigger it through value, transparency and bonuses, not fear.

## Key takeaways
- **Value over fear:** show what they *gain* by acting now (instant bonus module, early-bird coaching), not what they lose.
- **Social proof as soft urgency:** "Plus de 2000 élèves ont déjà rejoint" → reader doesn't want to lag the collective movement.
- **Transparent deadlines:** justify the limit ("inscriptions ferment vendredi car places limitées pour un suivi de qualité") → credible, not manipulative.
- **Exclusive bonuses > threats:** "session offerte avant minuit" beats "dépêchez-vous avant qu'il soit trop tard".
- **Align with benevolence:** "chaque semaine d'attente = une semaine sans client" — honest reminder that inaction delays results.

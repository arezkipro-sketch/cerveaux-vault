# Vendre un ebook 2026 — Summary

**One-liner:** Writing a PDF isn't the hard part — turning it into a product people buy/recommend is; near-100% margin asset that sells while you sleep.

## Key takeaways
- **Why ebooks:** ~zero production cost, instant global distribution, near-100% margin direct; also a funnel lever (free lead magnet vs paid positioning product). FR e-book market >€1.3B (SNE 2023); ~16M users by 2029 (Statista).
- **Topic:** solve one precise, searched, payable problem; niche beats generalist ("marathon en 12 semaines après 40 ans" > "guide du running"). 3 filters: verifiable demand, demonstrable expertise, logical monetisation.
- **Write:** structure as a kept promise (intro=problem, chapters=advances, conclusion=action plan); detailed TOC, examples, visuals, pro layout; beta-read with 3-5 target readers.
- **Price (FR):** €0-4.99 lead magnet · €5-19.99 practical guide · €20-97 written program; price signals value; test 2 weeks then adjust.
- **Platforms:** marketplaces (Amazon KDP, 30-65% fee, visibility) vs direct (Gumroad/Podia/Systeme.io, keep 90-97%, owns client). Combine both.
- **Sales page:** benefit title → problem hook → solution+results → social proof → repeated CTA → guarantee. Users scan in F (Nielsen) → strongest args top-left.

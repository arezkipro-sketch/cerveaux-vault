# Les 3 émotions qui déclenchent l'achat — Summary

**One-liner:** Buying is emotion-led; three emotions in sequence — security → desire → urgency — reproduce the brain's natural decision path.

## Key takeaways
- **Security** (reassure): answers "am I making the right choice?". Levers: satisfait-ou-remboursé guarantee, authentic testimonials, social proof. Lowers anxiety → readier to act.
- **Desire** (project a better future): the prospect imagines life after buying; activates dopamine. Benefit-promise ("vous signerez votre premier client en 30 jours") >> neutral info ("le programme dure 4 semaines"). Must be sincere, not unrealistic.
- **Urgency** (act now): fastest trigger, tied to FOMO. Time-limited offer, rare spots, early bonus — but must be *real*; fake "exceptional" promos erode trust.
- **Combine in order:** reassure (guarantees) → inspire (desirable projection) → push (urgency). Accompany the prospect, don't force.

## Notable claims (with locations)
- "Ce n'est pas le produit en lui-même qui séduit, mais ce qu'il fait ressentir." — *(§intro)*
- Example: an online trainer doubled signups via guarantee (security) + specific transformation (desire) + 20-seat cap (urgency). — *(§exemples)*

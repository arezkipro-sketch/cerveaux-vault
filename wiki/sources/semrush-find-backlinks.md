# Trouver et vérifier les backlinks — Summary

**One-liner:** Backlinks = links from other sites to yours; you need them to rank. A backlink analysis measures their quantity + quality and surfaces link opportunities.

## Key takeaways
- **Backlink analysis** measures who links to you (and competitors), the quality of linking domains, the [[maillage-interne|anchor text]] used, and follow vs nofollow (nofollow doesn't pass ranking strength).
- **Why:** find new backlink opportunities — replicate what works (e.g. if case studies attract links, make more), and chase high-quality links. Do it regularly.
- **Find backlinks** via dedicated checker tools (own site + competitors) — competitor link gaps are prime targets.

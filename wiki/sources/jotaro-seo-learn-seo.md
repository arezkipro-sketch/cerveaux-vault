# Where to Learn SEO — Resource Guide

**One-liner:** @JotaroSeo's personal resource list for learning SEO — covering a foundational book, French and English YouTube channels, and SEO blogs; includes his own blog optimize-ton-seo.fr.

## Key takeaways
- **Author's background**: Souleymane (Jotaro), 3 years in SEO at time of writing (2024), worked on e-commerce, blogs, affiliate sites with 1k–50k monthly traffic.
- **Book**: "Réussir son référencement web" by Olivier Andrieu (2021 edition) — covers SEO concepts/lexicon and advanced techniques like international, local, predictive SEO.
- **YouTube — French**: Abondanceseo (@abondance_com), SEMRush France (@semrush_fr).
- **YouTube — English**: Ahrefs channel, Jaume Ros (uncommon strategies).
- **Recent discoveries**: Paul Vengeons (@VengeonsP, beginner-friendly), Romain Pirotte (@WoodsDolls, advanced).
- **SEO Blogs**: SEM JUICE, semrush.com, semji.com, smartkeyword.io, noiise.com, Blogdumoderateur.com, optimize-ton-seo.fr (his own blog).

# Persuasive Writing (15 techniques)

**Thesis:** Persuasion is a learnable, structured craft built on how humans actually decide — not a gift. **Persuasive writing is the broad skill (articles, emails, speeches); copywriting is its sales-oriented application.** Goal: write *more accurately*, not more. → [[jeremy-allard-ecriture-persuasive]]

## The 15 techniques
1. Promise a clear benefit up front · 2. Back claims with precise verifiable facts · 3. Plant pre-agreement · 4. Match persuasion to the reader's existing agreement · 5. Simplify arguments (analogy/metaphor) · 6. Lead to "yes" by small steps · 7. Use proven frameworks (AIDA…) · 8. Choose the right tone · 9. Write to one individual · 10. Establish authority with concrete detail · 11. Raise & reverse objections early · 12. Write in the audience's language · 13. Use high-impact words · 14. Loop back & reinforce · 15. Close strong with an explicit CTA.

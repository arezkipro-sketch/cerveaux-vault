# Copywriting vs Rédaction Web

**Thesis:** They are complementary, not rival. Web writing *attracts and informs* (SEO traffic); copywriting *converts* (action). Best results come from combining both across the funnel — and the two overlap precisely at "SEO copywriting".

## Comparison

| Axis | Copywriting | Rédaction web |
|---|---|---|
| **Goal** | Convert reader → client/prospect | Inform, educate, rank in search |
| **Primary metric** | Conversion rate | Organic traffic / visibility |
| **Core techniques** | Catchy headlines, benefits-over-features, repeated CTAs, psychological levers | Keyword + tag optimisation, internal linking, clear hierarchy, factual completeness |
| **Typical assets** | Sales page, email, ad | Blog post, info page, product sheet |
| **Leans on** | [[copywriting-psychology]], [[copywriting-frameworks]] | [[keyword-research]], [[maillage-interne]], [[article-structure]] |

## How they combine
- An SEO blog post (web writing) embeds copywriting to convert readers (newsletter signup, purchase).
- A sales page (copywriting) is SEO-optimised so it ranks.
- Funnel view: **SEO/web writing fills the top (traffic) → copywriting closes the bottom (conversion).** This is the bridge between this vault's SEO cluster and copywriting cluster.

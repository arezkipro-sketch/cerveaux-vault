# Profound

**Definition:** Enterprise AI-Visibility platform (tryprofound.com) for deep intelligence on how AI platforms talk about a brand — beyond citation counts → [[ai-visibility-tools]].

## What we know
- Analyzes **context** of each mention: **Sentiment** (positive/negative framing), **competitive mentions** (who appears alongside), **Authority** (which topic clusters you're seen as a reference in) → [[ai-visibility-tools]].
- Highly customizable: tailored dashboards, internal-system integration, custom report formats, alerts when a brand disappears from AI answers; regulated-industry tracking → [[ai-visibility-tools]].
- High annual pricing scaling with brands/queries/customization — not for small business → [[ai-visibility-tools]].

## Relations
- Enterprise tier of [[geo-tools]]; competes with [[scrunchai]] (specialized) and in-suite [[semrush]]/[[ahrefs]]/[[ubersuggest]].

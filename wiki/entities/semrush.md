# Semrush

**Type:** B2B SaaS company — SEO and digital marketing intelligence platform. Dual role in this wiki: (1) source of AI SEO research studies and data, (2) subject of product promotion throughout their blog content.

## What we know

### As a research publisher + SEO knowledge base
- **72 source pages** (`semrush-*`) ingested — far beyond the original 14 AI-SEO studies. Now also covers: technical SEO (off-page, backlinks, toxic links, redirects, mobile, sitemap, site health, migration), keyword research (intent, organic, question, negative, PAA), local SEO (citations, near-me, GBP posts, Alexa), analytics (GA4, GSC), content (topic clusters, roadmap), SEM/ads/display, GEO/AI-search tools + engines, and marketing tool listicles (catalogued in [[marketing-tools-landscape]]).

### Commercial bias note
All Semrush blog content promotes Semrush tools (AI Visibility Toolkit, Site Audit, Position Tracking, ContentShake). Data and frameworks are real and reusable; tool recommendations are self-serving. Extract the insights; product mentions are navigation noise.

### Methodology transparency
- Studies based on Semrush's own data (hundreds of millions of keywords, SERP tracking).
- Some studies don't disclose exact methodology in blog summaries.
- Cross-reference with [[alex-groberman]] (Ahrefs-based data) and [[jespernissenseo-ai-traffic-stats]] (SE Ranking data) where relevant.

### AI Visibility tool positioning
- In a third-party (NP Digital) comparison, Semrush's AI-Visibility add-on offers a **visibility score** (like domain authority) and ties AI citations to backlinks/rankings, but uses **agency pricing that limits projects** — costly past ~30 brands → [[ai-visibility-tools]]. Peer/competitor of [[ahrefs]], [[ubersuggest]], [[profound]], [[scrunchai]].

# Nicki Krawczyk

**Definition:** Copywriter and copywriting educator; founder of **Filthy Rich Writer** (filthyrichwriter.com) and host of the *Build Your Copywriting Business* podcast. Publishes career-focused, craft-focused English copywriting content and a copywriting course (Comprehensive Copywriting Academy). → [[frw-what-is-cta]]

## What we know
- Runs the FRW blog's "Copy Q&A" series — short craft explainers (CTA, benefits-vs-features, creative brief, copy doc). → [[frw-what-is-cta]]
- Co-hosts the podcast with Kate Sitarz (see [[frw-ep280-making-herstory]]).
- Editorial stance: copywriting is a learnable, structured skill and a viable career — career/business angle, not just craft.

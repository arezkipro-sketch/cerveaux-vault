# YouTube

**Definition:** Google's video-sharing platform. Its [[youtube-algorithm]] (ML recommendation system) governs visibility across Search, recommendations, the Trending tab, and **Shorts** (its short-form format competing with TikTok and Instagram Reels) → [[youtube-algorithme]].

## What we know
- In 2025, Shorts is one of the platform's main strategic axes, with its own rapid-interaction algorithm → [[youtube-algorithme]].
- Visibility is earned via watch time, retention, [[click-through-rate]], and engagement → [[youtube-algorithm]].

## Relations
- Owned by Google; competitor surfaces: TikTok, Instagram Reels.
- Channel within [[video-marketing]] and [[social-media-strategy]].

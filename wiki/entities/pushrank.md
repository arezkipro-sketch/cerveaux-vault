# PushRank

**Definition:** An AI-powered SEO monitoring and recommendation tool under development by @JotaroSeo (Souleymane) and a team of senior developers (including @hamada_fahari and @ArbieuL). Positioned as a "best SEO tool you'll ever use" — aggregates daily site analysis, competitor monitoring, and personalized actionable recommendations.

## What we know
- Pre-launch as of 2026-06-09; waitlist open at pushrank.io/fr → [[raw/assets/jotaro-seo-ia-x-seo]], [[jotaro-seo-content-strategy]].
- **Features described**:
  - Daily site crawl + analysis (not weekly/monthly — every day).
  - Competitor monitoring: detects new content on competitor sites, position changes on target keywords, new backlinks acquired.
  - Personalized recommendations for the specific site + market + business model.
  - Accountability loop: next day, verifies if recommendations were implemented.
  - Library of SEO resources and tutorials (by Jotaro).
- Targets users who can't afford a full SEO consultant.
- Team: @JotaroSeo + senior devs @hamada_fahari, @ArbieuL → [[jotaro-seo-content-strategy]].

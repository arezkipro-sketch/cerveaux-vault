# Ahrefs

**Definition:** Established SEO platform best known for backlink analysis and competitive research; one of the most popular SEO tools. Added AI-Visibility tracking (filter by engine, time trends, competitor compare) → [[ai-visibility-tools]].

## What we know
- Standout: **links ↔ AI-citation correlation** — its large, frequently-updated backlink index shows which backlinked pages get cited most by AI, revealing what makes content "citable" → [[ai-visibility-tools]], reinforcing [[citation-absorption]].
- Weakness: premium pricing with per-project limits → costly at scale (5 clients fine, 50 heavy) → [[ai-visibility-tools]].

## Relations
- Category peer/competitor of [[semrush]], [[ubersuggest]] in [[geo-tools]] and [[marketing-tools-landscape]].
- Backlink focus ties to [[backlinks]], [[off-page-seo]].

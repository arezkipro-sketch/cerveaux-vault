# Memex

**Definition:** A conceptual personal knowledge device proposed by Vannevar Bush in 1945 — a private, actively curated store of documents linked by *associative trails*, where the connections between documents are as valuable as the documents themselves.

## What we know
- Proposed by Vannevar Bush in 1945 → [[llm-wiki-pattern]].
- Vision was private and actively curated — closer to a personal wiki than to what the web became → [[llm-wiki-pattern]].
- The unsolved part of Bush's vision was **who does the maintenance** of the associative trails → [[llm-wiki-pattern]].
- The [[persistent-wiki]] pattern is presented as the modern realization, with the LLM doing that maintenance → [[llm-wiki-pattern]].

## Relations
- Intellectual ancestor of [[persistent-wiki]].

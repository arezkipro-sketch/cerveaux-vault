# TikTok

**Definition:** Short-video platform that has become a **commercial discovery engine** — users search products directly on it, and it now influences Google search → [[search-everywhere-optimization]].

## What we know
- Optimize: trending hashtags, viral formats, influencer collaborations aligned to brand values; use TikTok Insights for data → [[search-everywhere-optimization]].
- ~40% of young users prefer IG/TikTok over Google for answers → [[search-everywhere-optimization]].

## Relations
- A channel of [[search-everywhere-optimization]] and [[social-media-strategy]]; peer of [[instagram]], [[pinterest]], [[youtube]] (Shorts competitor per [[youtube-algorithme]]).

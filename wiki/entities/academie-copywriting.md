# L'Académie™ (academiecopywriting.fr)

**Definition:** French-language copywriting training organisation founded by [[theo-rossi]], publishing educational blog content at academiecopywriting.fr and selling a paid copywriting course (application-gated via a "formulaire de candidature"). Signature guarantee: first client in 30 days or they supply one via a "réseau secret". → [[academie-copywriting-definition]], [[academie-effet-rarete]]

## What we know
- Publishes introductory copywriting blog articles in French. → [[academie-copywriting-definition]]
- Sells a complete copywriting formation; entry is by application form, not open enrolment. → [[academie-copywriting-definition]]
- Blog posts author byline appears as "L'académie" (house byline, not a named individual).

## Relations
- Source of the base [[copywriting]] definition page.
- One of several copywriting publishers in this vault alongside English-language ones behind [[copywriting-frameworks-2026]] and [[haddington-copywriting-psychology]].

## Tensions / open questions
- Commercial bias: blog content is a top-of-funnel lead magnet for paid training — treat claims as introductory, not independent.
- Founder = [[theo-rossi]] (contact theo@lecopywriter.fr); legal entity name still unstated.
